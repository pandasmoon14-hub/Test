# Stage 11C Batch C adjudication

## Decision

Batch C requires broader and deeper doctrine for:
- Natural Resources
- Bound Relics
- Relic Traits

without changing the stable stat block template.

## Promoted clarification rules

### Natural Resources
Must be broader than simple monster drops and must support creature, terrain, anomaly, and ruin extraction.

### Bound Relics
Must be defined as bonded growth-state items, not as a weapon-only subsystem.

### Relic Traits
Must be requirement-driven and category-based so future donor systems can add strange object and upgrade logic cleanly.

## Governing principle

Specific enough to feel real. Broad enough to survive later donor influx.
