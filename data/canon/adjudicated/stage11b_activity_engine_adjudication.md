# Stage 11B activity engine adjudication

## Decision

The activity engine packet must function as procedure, not just setting prose.

## Clarified doctrine

### Nexus Hub
A repeatable operations center with actionable return-cycle choices.

### Transit Seal
A strategic access key that creates negotiation, scarcity, and rivalry.

### Ascension Trial
A discrete claim-centered challenge node built around access, opposition, test, reward, and failure cost.

### Hunting Realm
A repeatable progression space optimized for pressure, encounters, and harvest.

### Expedition Site
A tactical or unstable objective space distinct from both Trials and Realms.

### Stronghold Phase
The between-expedition phase where gains become persistent infrastructure.

## Practical rule

If a world-activity term cannot answer "what do players do here?" it is underdesigned.
