# RHBF Batch C Canon Candidates

## Strong candidates

1. Creature family determines at least part of the rules posture.
2. Stat blocks must preserve a fixed mechanical field order.
3. Harvest outputs are family-typed and feed multiple downstream systems.
4. Bound Relics grow through typed component investment rather than random loot scaling.
5. Ascendant Beasts provide a bridge between fauna ecology and person-level antagonists or allies.

## Open adjudications

1. Final family terminology set for Astra taxonomy.
2. Whether Presence Pressure remains the permanent replacement for Aura.
3. Whether Natural Treasure remains universal or splits into Astra-specific treasure classes.
