# RHBF Batch C — Taxonomy, Harvest, and Bound Relics v0.1

## Taxonomy doctrine

Creature type should predict more than flavor.
It should predict:
- likely damage interactions
- likely harvest categories
- likely relic trait compatibility
- likely encounter posture

## Stat block doctrine

Never flatten donor stat blocks into prose.
The stat block is itself a mechanical artifact.

## Harvest doctrine

Harvest outputs stay grouped by family because that is what allows later systems
like cooking, alchemy, relic feeding, trophies, and trade to feel systematic.

## Bound relic doctrine

A Bound Relic:
- is owner-linked
- persists as an identity-bearing item
- gains Relic Traits through typed components and ingredients
- may solve utility, combat, navigation, defense, or factional problems

## Immediate canon candidates

- Bound Relic
- Relic Trait
- Resonant Feeding
- Ascendant Beast
- Presence Pressure
- essence core
