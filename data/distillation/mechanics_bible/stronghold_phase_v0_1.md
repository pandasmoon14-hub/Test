# Astra Stronghold Phase v0.1

## Purpose
Represents the return-home management phase between expeditions.

## Stronghold Phase actions
- repair and reinforce
- process harvested materials
- manage structures and upgrades
- resolve local events
- allocate resources
- answer political or environmental threats

## Design law
Astra progression should affect both the Ascendant and the civilization or settlement they support.
