# RHBF merged donor rulebook packet v0.1

This file consolidates the first Astra-facing donor synthesis across RHBF Batches A, B, and C.

## Player chassis

Astra Ascendants use a six-attribute chassis:
Vessel, Core, Flow, Insight, Resonance, and Intent.

Defense is handled by three Resistances:
Core Resistance, Flow Resistance, and Resonance Resistance.

Progression uses Stage and Degree, with Lineage and Soul acting as breakthrough gates.

Characters advance through Paths and Techniques, with Techniques occupying a nine-slot architecture across offensive, utility, movement, and ultimate roles.

## World activity loop

Ascendants operate through a Nexus Hub and access opportunity sites using Transit Seals.

Activity centers on:
- Ascension Trials
- Hunting Realms
- faction contracts
- expedition sites
- Stronghold Phase growth between expeditions

## Creature and relic loop

Creatures are organized by family and carry mechanical posture through taxonomy.

Encounters feed harvest loops, harvest loops feed crafting and Bound Relic growth, and Bound Relics gain Relic Traits through component-driven advancement.

Ascendant Beasts bridge ecology, factional threat, and advanced NPC design.

## Open doctrine

The final Astra framework still requires adjudication around reserve terminology, body-slot metaphysics, aura naming, fate systems, and technology doctrine.
