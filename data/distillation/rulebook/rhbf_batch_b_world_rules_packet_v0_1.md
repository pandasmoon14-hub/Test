# RHBF Batch B — Astra World Rules Packet v0.1

## Core loop
Ascendants cycle through five major activity states:
1. prepare in a Nexus Hub
2. secure access through Transit Seals, contracts, or faction leverage
3. enter Ascension Trials, Hunting Realms, or Expedition Sites
4. resolve encounters, claims, rewards, and consequences
5. return to Stronghold Phase for upgrades, defense, and strategic planning

## Nexus Hub
A Nexus Hub is neutral territory where Ascendants gather intelligence, secure contracts, buy access, acquire transit rights, and negotiate with factions.

## Transit Seals
Transit Seals are explicit access keys. They gate entry into sites, realms, and trials. They may be purchased, earned, claimed, or granted by faction authority.

## Ascension Trials
Ascension Trials are modular challenge nodes. Each trial should expose:
- access method
- location or realm type
- urgency
- principal claimants or rivals
- objective
- reward profile
- aesthetic signature

## Hunting Realms
Hunting Realms are repeatable progression zones with rank-banding, travel cadence, encounter checks, and harvest/reward loops.

## Faction Pressure
Factions should create ongoing pressure through contracts, competition, ideology, denied access, and resource claims.

## Stronghold Phase
After expeditions, Ascendants return to Stronghold Phase to repair, fortify, expand infrastructure, and convert expedition gains into durable strategic advantage.
