# RHBF Batch B — Canon Candidates

## Candidate laws
1. Ascendants require a stable hub-and-expedition loop, not just abstract progression.
2. Access gating should be explicit and materialized through keys, rights, seals, or authorizations.
3. Trials should be modular and generator-friendly.
4. Repeatable hunting zones should exist as progression infrastructure.
5. Factions should actively alter opportunity costs and access.
6. Progression should feed back into stronghold-scale upgrades.

## Open caution
Do not import RHBF source ideology or anti-technology posture into Astra canon without review.
