# Stage 1-3 canon candidates

## Candidate laws

1. Astra uses six core attributes with the established Astra naming layer.
2. Astra uses three primary resistances aligned to Core, Flow, and Resonance.
3. Astra progression is multi-axis and includes Stage, Degree, Lineage, and Soul.
4. Paths are the class-equivalent structure and Domains gate Techniques.
5. Techniques are the standard power unit and use structured action/range/usage formatting.
6. Astra world play should revolve around hubs, access keys, trials, hunting zones, and home-base management.
7. Bound Relics are the preferred evolving-weapon framework.
8. Creature taxonomy should carry mechanical meaning rather than being purely descriptive.
9. Harvest and natural treasures should feed directly into crafting, relic growth, or character advancement.
