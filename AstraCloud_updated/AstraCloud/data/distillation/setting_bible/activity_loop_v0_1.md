# Astra Activity Loop v0.1

## Core play loop
1. Prepare in Nexus Hub
2. Acquire or spend Transit Seal
3. Enter Ascension Trial or Hunting Realm
4. Resolve hazards, enemies, and objectives
5. Harvest materials and claim rewards
6. Return to Stronghold Phase
7. Respond to Pressure Events and faction fallout
8. Advance Stage, Degree, Lineage, Soul, relics, and holdings

## Design law
Ascendants should always have a reason to leave safety, and always have a reason to come back.
