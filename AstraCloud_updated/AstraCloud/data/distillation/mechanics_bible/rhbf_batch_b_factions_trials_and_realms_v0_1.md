# RHBF Batch B — Factions, Trials, and Realms v0.1

## Faction Pressure Engine
Astra factions should not be passive lore wallpaper. They should:
- offer contracts
- gate or grant access
- contest sites
- generate pressure events
- shape the cost of inaction

## Trial structure
An Ascension Trial is strongest when it contains:
- a clear entrance condition
- a rank expectation
- a competing claimant
- a governing hazard or theme
- a completion trigger
- a lasting reward or consequence

## Hunting Realm structure
A Hunting Realm should support repeated entry and escalating mastery. It should define:
- region type
- expected rank band
- travel cadence
- encounter cadence
- harvest opportunities
- exhaustion or depletion pressure

## Stronghold implications
The world loop should always feed back into durable growth:
- defense
- transit infrastructure
- repositories / archives
- crafting or refinement capacity
- faction leverage
