## Explicitly excluded from accepted terms

These remain donor-local or unresolved and should not be treated as accepted Astra-facing lexicon.

- `Edge`
- technomancer `Resonance` as a donor term
- exact `Matrix`, `host`, `IC`, and `spider` stack vocabulary
- exact `Essence` law and decimal cost semantics
- exact `Mr. Johnson` / fixer slang
- exact corporate, underworld, and legal slang specific to Shadowrun
