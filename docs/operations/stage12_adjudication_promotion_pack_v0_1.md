# Stage 12 — Adjudication and Promotion Pack v0.1

This stage performs the first explicit doctrinal promotion pass after the live conversion seed layer.

## Goal

Convert the accumulated provisional queue into one of three buckets:

1. **Promoted canonical doctrine**
2. **Preserve-for-traceability only**
3. **Blocked from Astra canon**

## Why this matters

Without adjudication, a donor stream gradually becomes secret canon.
This pack prevents that by making the decisions explicit.

## Decisions made in this pass

### Promoted
- Presence Pressure
- Fate
- Stronghold Phase
- Technique Repository
- Gate Array
- Meridian Reserve
- Meridian Channels

### Preserve for traceability, not canon
- Aether
- Essence
- RHBF proper nouns
- source faction names
- source class names

### Blocked from Astra canon
- raw Aura as the preferred canonical power metric
- raw Luck as the preferred canonical fate term
- donor anti-technology absolutism
- silent source cosmology carryover
