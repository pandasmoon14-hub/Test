# D03-A D20 Backlash Trigger Terminology Amendment

Version: v0.1  
Status: Compatibility amendment  
Applies to: D03 Power Economy doctrine pack  
Reason: D02 changed to locked d20-first resolution with Astra five-state margin outcomes.

## Purpose

This amendment updates D03 backlash-trigger terminology so D03 remains compatible with the locked D02 d20-first resolution architecture.

This is not a regeneration of D03. It is a narrow compatibility override for older D02 outcome labels that appeared in D03 backlash language.

## Compatibility problem

D03 contains older failure-label language such as:

- `Failure with Consequence`;
- `Severe Failure`;
- `Critical Failure`.

Those labels should no longer be treated as active D02 outcome names.

The locked D02 outcome states are:

| D02 outcome | Margin guide |
|---|---:|
| Ascendant | +10 or higher |
| Clear | 0 to +9 |
| Fractured | -1 to -4 |
| Receded | -5 to -9 |
| Sundered | -10 or lower |

D02 also defines:

- natural 20 as a critical opportunity, not automatic impossible success;
- natural 1 as a complication flag, not automatic failure.

## Amendment rule

Where D03 references older failure labels for backlash, overdraw, resource rupture, instability, or unsafe power use, read those labels through the following replacement doctrine.

### Replacement backlash trigger language

Backlash may occur on:

- **Sundered** results;
- **Receded** results when the declared risk profile, source-local rule, unstable carrier, or unsafe Power Economy state includes backlash;
- **Fractured** results when over-investment, overdraw, unsafe Expression use, unstable carrier pressure, or source-local backlash risk is present;
- **natural 1 complication flag** when the owner-file risk profile supports backlash;
- explicit overdraw;
- unsafe Expression attempt;
- overlimit use;
- incompatible carrier/economy contact;
- failed containment of volatile resource;
- route/power conflict validated by D06;
- harm/corruption interaction validated by D07;
- actor substrate instability validated by D08;
- object/platform instability validated by D09;
- source-local backlash trigger retained under boundary.

## Non-replacement clauses

This amendment does not change D03’s ownership.

D03 still owns:

- Power Economy resources;
- carriers;
- pools;
- charges;
- fuel;
- reserves;
- overdraw;
- instability;
- recharge logic;
- resource-side backlash triggers.

D03 does not own:

- d20 resolution procedure;
- final outcome-state definitions;
- D02 margin bands;
- damage/harm mechanics;
- actor substrate;
- object-state;
- world-state.

## Outcome interpretation for D03

D03 should interpret D02 results as follows when Power Economy risk is active:

| D02 result | D03 Power Economy reading |
|---|---|
| Ascendant | Declared cost applies; resource expression may be efficient, stabilized, amplified, or clean if owner rules allow. |
| Clear | Declared cost applies; intended resource expression resolves normally. |
| Fractured | Declared cost plus strain, inefficiency, partial resource leak, instability tick, reduced output, or extra cost. |
| Receded | Resource spent or partly spent; expression fails or underperforms; exposure, instability, or backlash risk may occur if declared. |
| Sundered | Severe resource failure, backlash, overdraw rupture, instability spike, carrier damage, or owner-file routed consequence. |
| Natural 20 | Critical opportunity for efficiency, stabilization, clean channeling, reduced cost, or improved expression if lawful. |
| Natural 1 | Complication flag: possible instability, waste, mischannel, overdraw stress, or backlash where risk posture supports it. |

## Source-local boundary

If a donor source uses a local backlash table, mishap table, corruption die, spell failure roll, psionic strain roll, reactor instability roll, or similar subsystem, it must receive one of the usual lawful outcomes:

- direct D03/D02 mapping;
- normalized D03/D02 mapping;
- source-local retained subsystem;
- quarantine;
- escalation.

It does not become Astra’s universal backlash rule.

## Acceptance criteria

D03 is compatible with locked D02 when:

1. old failure labels are no longer treated as active D02 outcomes;
2. D03 references Fractured, Receded, Sundered, natural 1, and owner-file risk posture instead;
3. backlash remains tied to declared risk, unsafe use, overdraw, instability, or source-local boundary;
4. D02 remains the resolution owner;
5. D03 remains the Power Economy owner.
