## Purpose

This D05 pack defines Astra Ascension's competency layer: what actors can practice, know, train, research, specialize, synthesize, teach, and use as a lawful basis for action. It connects D02 resolution, D03 Power Economies and Expressions, D04 advancement/proof, and later D06 Path/Class/Dao design.

D05 is not a short skill list and not a hidden class system. It is the substrate that lets practiced life, profession, study, experimentation, mastery, and nonclass growth matter mechanically without allowing unsupported power declaration.

## Doctrine status

This pack is design doctrine, not final sourcebook prose and not final runtime schema. It should later feed schema work, canon consolidation, conversion routing, runtime implementation, and play-facing examples. It should not be treated as live-play improvisation authority by itself.

After acceptance, perform a D03 amendment pass and a D04 amendment pass before D06 begins. D06 depends on D05 because Path/Class/Dao design cannot safely distinguish route identity from skill, profession, method, technique candidate, Expression candidate, research output, or advancement-track pressure until D05 is stable.

## Ownership boundary

D05 owns competency architecture; skill, profession, knowledge, training, research, mastery, specialization, method, synthesis, teaching doctrine; relevance handling for checks; D05-owned advancement tracks; routing grammar for non-D05 advancement tracks; and record-shape obligations for D05 structures.

D05 does not own D02 roll math; D03 cost/carrier/Expression mechanics; D04 proof sufficiency, tier authorization, Awakening, breakthrough, capstone, or Ascension Access; D06 Path/Class/Dao, Technique, Domain, or concept authority; D07 harm/corruption/mutation/recovery; D08 companion/spirit/AI/actor-state; D09 item/relic/platform; D10 faction/territory/world-state; final runtime database schema; canon sourcebook prose; or live-play GM behavior.

## Core D05 principle

Competency is Astra's learned-capability substrate. It determines what an actor can notice, attempt, interpret, improve, teach, research, formalize, and synthesize. It can modify action resolution and create lawful action vectors, but it does not by itself grant Expressions, Power Economies, Path/Class/Dao identity, supernatural authority, titles, faction rank, item evolution, companion evolution, territory authority, or capstone status.

## Pre-generation fixes incorporated

This pack incorporates the accepted risk-audit fixes:

- D05 is capability substrate, not universal advancement authority.
- Research creates candidates, not final powers.
- Synthesis creates structured outputs, not stacked bonuses.
- Professions create proof and pressure, not automatic classes.
- Advancement Tracks route by owner, not surface name.
- Methods remain below Techniques and Expressions unless handed off.
- Durable records require thresholds.
- Donor-shaped terms remain pressure signals unless formally promoted.
- D03/D04 amendment queues are mandatory before D06.
