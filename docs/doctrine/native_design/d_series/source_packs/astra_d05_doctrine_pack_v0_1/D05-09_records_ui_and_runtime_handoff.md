# D05-09 Records, UI, and Runtime Handoff

**Astra Ascension Doctrine Pack:** D05 — Competency, Profession, Knowledge, Training, Research, and Synthesis  
**Version:** v0.1  
**Generated:** 2026-05-31  
**Status:** Design doctrine generated from accepted D05 decisions.  
**Scope rule:** D05 defines capability substrate. It does not authorize final Expression mechanics, Power Economy math, Path/Class/Dao identity, breakthrough outcomes, item evolution, companion evolution, faction authority, territory control, canon sourcebook text, runtime database schema, or live-play GM behavior.

## Design question

What must Astra record so competencies, professions, training, research, methods, synthesis, and relevance remain usable without excessive bookkeeping or layer collapse?

## Core record law

D05 uses a tiered record model. Ordinary competency use may remain ephemeral. Competency interactions become durable when they affect long-term state, proof, training, research, methods, synthesis, visibility, route pressure, teaching authority, degradation, source-local status, Development Pick eligibility, or owner-file handoff. D05 record families preserve capability substrate without replacing runtime schemas, canon records, D03/D04/D06 authority, or live narration.

## Record tiers

- **Ephemeral:** temporary check support; not normally preserved. Use for ordinary minor skill use, flavor-only support, failed relevance claims without consequence, trivial practice.
- **Session-relevant:** preserved during current play when it may matter soon. Use when relevance changed a roll, consequence was reduced, assessment revealed important information, training/research made progress, a method seed might be emerging, claimed competency was harmful, source-local competency appeared, or future proof may depend on it.
- **Durable:** actor/character state record. Required when long-term capability, proof, training, research, method, synthesis, visibility, route pressure, teaching, degradation, source-local status, Development Pick eligibility, or owner handoff changes.

## Durable thresholds

Durable records are required when competency rank changes, visibility changes, training/research opens/completes/stalls/deforms, method state changes, specialization appears, synthesis becomes recorded, proof or route pressure is generated, owner handoff is needed, competency degrades/corrupts/mutates/externalizes/source-localizes, teaching authority is granted, Development Pick eligibility changes, or Awakening/breakthrough/capstone proof is affected.

## Record families

D05 includes Competency Record, Profession Record, Knowledge Record, Method Record, Training Project Record, Research Track Record, Synthesis Record, Relevance Record, Teaching Authority Record, and Owner-Handoff Record. These are doctrine handoff structures, not final database schemas.

## Family summaries

Competency Records track name, family, subtype, rank, progress, visibility, relationships, tools, methods, specializations, training, research, proof, source-local status, and handoffs.

Profession Records track profession identity, active status, duration, tools/workspace/access, social standing, methods, apprenticeships, proof, procedures, projects, route pressure, Awakening relevance, and boundaries.

Knowledge Records track field, rank, source, reliability, scope, languages/scripts, tags, research links, interpretive relevance, false/outdated flags, and hidden/partial states.

Method Records track method state, transformation state, parent competencies, use case, conditions, tools/anchors, effect summary, limits, risks, relevance profile, proof/training/research links, handoff candidates, teaching eligibility.

Training Project Records track target, rank, target output, mode, access, teacher/tool/environment refs, inputs, risk, progress, output, proof, failure/deformation, handoff.

Research Track Records track question, desired outcome, basis, missing basis, modes, inputs, risk, progress, output class, handoffs, validation, proof, source-local status.

Synthesis Records track components, interaction type, state, functional relationship, effects, limits, risks, outputs, handoffs, validation.

Relevance Records track important check support when it affects proof, training, research, route pressure, consequence, or access.

Teaching Authority Records track source, target, authority level, conditions, compatible learners, restrictions, proof, lineage/institution pressure, and D10 handoff.

Owner-Handoff Records exist when D05 points outside itself.

## Visibility and fairness

Hidden, latent, source-local, harmful, degraded, and backend-only states may exist, but they should surface through discovery, assessment, training, research, consequence, proof events, teacher feedback, owner-file reveal, or narrative exposure backed by state. Hidden states must not become arbitrary punishment.

## UI grouping

Future UI should group by Competencies, Professions, Methods, Training, Research, Synthesis, Teaching, Growth Links, and Local/Special. Do not display every doctrine category flatly.

## Runtime handoff principles

D05 records are not model memory, final canon, or freeform permission. They are actor-state support and advancement evidence. D05 can produce candidates and pressure, not final cross-system authority. Runtime should validate state changes through owner files. UI should present useful summaries, not raw doctrine complexity.

## Doctrine shapes, not schemas

All record examples are doctrine handoff structures. Later runtime/schema work will decide exact field names, database tables, validation code, persistence model, indexing, and UI.
