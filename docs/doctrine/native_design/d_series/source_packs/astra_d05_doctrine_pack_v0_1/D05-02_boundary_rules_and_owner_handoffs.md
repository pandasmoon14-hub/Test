# D05-02 Boundary Rules and Owner Handoffs

**Astra Ascension Doctrine Pack:** D05 — Competency, Profession, Knowledge, Training, Research, and Synthesis  
**Version:** v0.1  
**Generated:** 2026-05-31  
**Status:** Design doctrine generated from accepted D05 decisions.  
**Scope rule:** D05 defines capability substrate. It does not authorize final Expression mechanics, Power Economy math, Path/Class/Dao identity, breakthrough outcomes, item evolution, companion evolution, faction authority, territory control, canon sourcebook text, runtime database schema, or live-play GM behavior.

## Design question

How does Astra distinguish Skills, Professions, Backgrounds, Knowledge Fields, Methods, Techniques, Expressions, Training Tracks, Research Tracks, Advancement Tracks, and Path/Class/Dao structures so donor packaging does not decide ownership?

## Core boundary law

Skills are narrow practiced capabilities. Professions are life-pattern competencies. Backgrounds are origin contexts that may seed competencies. Knowledge Fields support understanding and inference but do not author world truth. Methods are repeatable competency applications below Techniques. Techniques and Expressions hand off to D06/D03 when they gain formal activation, cost, effect, route, or power structure. Training improves known or discoverable capability. Research converts uncertainty into theory, prototype, blueprint, or lawful action vector. Competency may create route pressure but not route identity.

## Boundary rules

### Skill

A Skill is a bounded practiced capability. It may affect roll posture, modifiers, information yield, action-vector legality, cost, consequence, training eligibility, proof records, and method creation. It should not by itself grant route identity, supernatural effect, technique tree, or Path/Class/Dao package.

### Profession

A Profession is a practiced life pattern made of skills, tools, routines, social role, problem-solving habits, material familiarity, and proof generation. It can generate proof clusters, Awakening vectors, route pressure, Development Pick targets, tool access, recipe/project access, social standing, apprenticeships, methods, title seeds, and faction or market access. It becomes Path/Class/Dao pressure only when D04 proof and D06 route formalization support it.

### Background

A Background describes origin context. It may seed starting competencies, contacts, tools, debts, languages, reputation, social access, and proof seeds. It does not replace active competency. A character from a fishing village is not automatically a practiced fisher unless the record supports practice.

### Knowledge Field

A Knowledge Field is understanding, not fact authority. It supports identification, assessment, research, crafting, dialogue, interpretation, hidden-mechanic discovery, route recognition, and false-assumption avoidance. A character with ancient-law knowledge may interpret legal relics or infer institutional patterns; they cannot declare a matching empire exists unless D10 or source-local context supports it.

### Method

A Method is a repeatable application of competency. It can become a training form, research output, profession signature, route seed, Technique candidate, or Expression candidate. It is not automatically a Technique or Expression.

### Technique

A Technique is more formalized than a Method. Once it has defined activation, effect, cost, scope, risk, or route relation, it begins handing off to D06 and sometimes D03. D05 owns training support, not formal Technique authority.

### Expression

An Expression is a retained, activated, or formalized power-capability governed by D03/D06. D05 supports research, training, safe practice, known-but-not-inscribed understanding, refinement requirements, and lawful action-vector formation. It does not authorize final Expression mechanics.

### Training Track

A Training Track improves an existing or discoverable capability through practice. It may output competency progress, method refinement, proof, reliability, safer posture, Development Pick eligibility, rank increase, or teaching eligibility. It does not answer unknown questions; Research does.

### Research Track

A Research Track investigates something not yet fully known. It may output theory, blueprint, recipe, prototype, failed hypothesis, lawful action vector, warning, requirement list, route possibility, Expression candidate, project requirement, or source-local limitation. It does not automatically grant the desired result.

### Competency vs Advancement Track

A Competency is a learned-capability structure. An Advancement Track is any durable growth lane. Some tracks are D05-owned competencies; many route to D03, D04, D06, D07, D08, D09, D10, source-local handling, or escalation.

### Route pressure, not route identity

Competency can create route pressure but not route identity. D05 records the competency basis. D04 records proof. D06 formalizes route identity.

## Conversion tests

If donor material improves practiced ability, map to Skill or Competency. If it describes job, vocation, service history, trade, or life role, map to Profession or Background depending on active practice. If it describes origin context, map to Background. If it describes stored understanding, map to Knowledge Field. If it describes learning over time, map to Training Track. If it describes discovering formulas, methods, blueprints, or theories, map to Research Track. If it has activation, cost, effect, scope, resource, or power behavior, hand off to D03/D06. If it grants formal identity, archetype, class feature, domain authority, or path structure, hand off to D06. If it handles level, title, proof, milestone, or breakthrough, hand off to D04.

## Examples

Hammer Rhythm as D05 Method reduces cracking when forging difficult metal. Hammer Rhythm as D06 Technique candidate becomes a formal forge art. Hammer Rhythm as D03 Expression candidate uses an economy to alter metal behavior beyond ordinary craft.

Fisher is a Profession. Depth Reading is a Method. Impossible-line research may be a Research Track. Repeated proof creates D04 Awakening pressure. D06 may later formalize Far Fisher. No D05 record automatically creates the route.
