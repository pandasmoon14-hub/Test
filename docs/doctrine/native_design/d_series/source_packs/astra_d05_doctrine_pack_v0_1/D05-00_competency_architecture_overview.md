# D05-00 Competency Architecture Overview

**Astra Ascension Doctrine Pack:** D05 — Competency, Profession, Knowledge, Training, Research, and Synthesis  
**Version:** v0.1  
**Generated:** 2026-05-31  
**Status:** Design doctrine generated from accepted D05 decisions.  
**Scope rule:** D05 defines capability substrate. It does not authorize final Expression mechanics, Power Economy math, Path/Class/Dao identity, breakthrough outcomes, item evolution, companion evolution, faction authority, territory control, canon sourcebook text, runtime database schema, or live-play GM behavior.

## Design question

What is a competency in Astra Ascension, what does D05 own, and how does the competency layer support checks, professions, research, training, proof, methods, synthesis, and future runtime state without becoming a hidden class or power system?

## Working answer

Astra uses a Layered Competency Architecture. Competency is the umbrella learned-capability substrate. It includes Skills, Professions, Knowledge Fields, Training Tracks, Research Tracks, Mastery Ranks, Specializations, Methods, Synthesis structures, and Teaching Authority.

Competency may affect what an actor can attempt; how a check is modified; what information a result produces; what costs or consequences change; what training paths are available; what research questions can be pursued; what methods can form; what proof can be generated; what route pressure can emerge; and what owner-file handoffs are required.

Competency does not authorize final powers or route identity.

## D05 ownership

D05 owns the learned-capability layer. It defines the difference between skill, profession, background, knowledge, method, training, research, mastery, specialization, synthesis, and teaching. It defines how these structures affect checks through relevance, how they improve through training, how they investigate unknowns through research, how mastery produces methods and route pressure, and how nonclass advancement tracks route to owner files.

## D05 non-ownership

D05 does not own final authorization for D02 roll math; D03 Power Economies, Expressions, carrier math, cost, recharge, burden, or confluence; D04 proof sufficiency, Development Pick validation, Awakening Packages, tier rewards, breakthrough outcomes, capstone, or Ascension Access; D06 Path/Class/Dao identity, formal Techniques, Domains, concept authority, branches, or route fusion; D07 harm/corruption/mutation/recovery; D08 companions, spirits, AI, summons, actor-state changes, and bonds; D09 items, relics, platforms, vehicles, tools, external anchors; or D10 factions, reputation, territory, law, and world-state.

D05 may create support records, proof evidence, research outputs, candidates, and pressure for these systems. It does not resolve them alone.

## File-level doctrine law

D05 defines what an actor can practice, know, train, research, specialize, synthesize, teach, and use as capability substrate. D05 may create proof, route pressure, method seeds, research outputs, and owner-file candidates. D05 does not authorize final Expressions, Path/Class/Dao identity, Power Economy mechanics, breakthrough outcomes, item evolution, companion evolution, faction rank, territory authority, or world-state changes.

## Relationship to D02

D02 owns action resolution and d20 outcome structure. D05 owns how competencies become relevant to checks. D05 may tell D02 that a competency is directly relevant, that a profession has contextual relevance, that a knowledge field improves interpretation, that a method creates access relevance, or that prior training is harmful in context. D05 does not reopen D02 roll margins or outcome names.

## Relationship to D03

D03 owns Power Economies and Expressions. D05 supports D03 by recording training that improves Power Economy handling, research that creates Expression candidates, synthesis that suggests confluence or re-authoring, and methods that become Expression candidates. D05 cannot finalize Expression mechanics.

## Relationship to D04

D04 owns proof, advancement gates, Development Picks, Awakening, breakthrough, capstone, and Ascension Access. D05 feeds D04 through profession proof, training proof, research proof, mastery proof, method proof, synthesis proof, route pressure, and advancement-track evidence. D05 cannot decide proof sufficiency or grant advancement eligibility alone.

## Relationship to D06

D06 owns Path/Class/Dao, formal Techniques, Domains, conceptual authority, route identity, and route fusion. D05 can create route pressure, Technique candidates, profession-route seeds, method-to-technique handoffs, mastery evidence, research evidence, and synthesis evidence. D05 cannot formalize Path/Class/Dao identity.

## Relationship to D07-D10

D05 records support for later systems. D07 receives harmful methods, corrupted training, mutation pressure, injury recovery training, and stability risks. D08 receives companion handling, teaching, care, bond training, AI interface support, and nonstandard actor learning. D09 receives tool use, craft training, relic research, platform operation, externalized methods, and item-bound handoffs. D10 receives profession standing, institutional training, faction education, reputation-adjacent proof, teaching lineages, and world-facing professional effects.

## Layered model

D05's internal structure is: Competency, Skill, Profession, Knowledge Field, Training Track/Project, Research Track, Mastery, Specialization, Method, Synthesis, and Teaching Authority. These are capability substrates and records, not automatic powers.

## Corpus-scale justification

Astra must absorb a large donor corpus containing skill lists, broad domains, backgrounds, professions, crafting skills, knowledge systems, training systems, research clocks, martial arts, spell creation, power customization, titles, faction ranks, pet bonds, item refinement, territory growth, cultivation organs, divine progressions, cybernetic synchronization, and platform tracks. D05 must not flatten all of these into skills. It must route them lawfully.

## Required downstream amendments

D03 must later incorporate competency-supported Expression research, Power Economy training tracks, carrier/reservoir advancement-track recognition, methods and synthesis as Expression candidates, and D05 Research Tracks as re-authoring inputs.

D04 must later incorporate D05 records as proof sources, D05-owned Advancement Tracks, Development Pick targeting of competencies/training/research/methods/mastery/synthesis, profession and research proof in Awakening, and mastery/research/synthesis proof in Capstone.

D06 should not begin until those passes occur or are explicitly deferred.
