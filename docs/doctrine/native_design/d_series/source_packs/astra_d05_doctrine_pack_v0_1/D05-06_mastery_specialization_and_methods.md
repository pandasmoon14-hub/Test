# D05-06 Mastery, Specialization, and Methods

## Design question

What happens when a competency deepens beyond ordinary use, and how does it become mastery, specialization, method, teachable form, route pressure, Technique candidate, or Expression candidate?

## Core mastery law

Mastery is deep competency that can create formalization options. It may produce specializations, methods, teachable forms, proof weight, research acceleration, teaching authority, route pressure, and D03/D06 handoff candidates. Specializations remain bounded sub-competencies. Methods exist as Seed, Practiced, Formal, or Teachable states and may degrade, mutate, corrupt, externalize, or remain source-local. D05 methods do not become Techniques, Expressions, or Path/Class/Dao structures without owner-file validation.

## Mastery and Grandmastery

Mastery is deep command of a competency such that the actor can adapt it under pressure, teach principles, diagnose failure, create refinements, and generate proof. A Master blacksmith identifies hidden forging patterns, diagnoses blade failure, creates specialized methods, teaches apprentices, reduces waste, preserves tools, recognizes impossible materials, and generates proof.

Grandmastery is where competency can become world-facing, route-pressuring, or structurally transformative. A Grandmaster may create new methods, found a school, define a style, create rare proof, create lineages, support Awakening or capstone proof, and generate D06 route pressure under D04 review.

Transcendent competency is a record state for extreme mastery pressure. D05 records it; D04/D06 validate it.

## Specialization

A Specialization is a narrow branch within a competency. It is stronger inside scope and does not replace parent competency. Examples include Half-swording, Living Metal, Planar Rivers, Spirit-Wound Triage, Void Routes, Criminal Mediation, Cursed Relics, Mech Docking. It may affect relevance, information, cost, consequence, training speed, method access, and proof quality inside its scope.

## Method states

| State | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Method Seed | Pattern emerging but not reliable. |
| Practiced Method | Actor can repeat under favorable conditions. |
| Formal Method | Defined trigger, use case, limits, risk, training path. |
| Teachable Method | Actor can transmit under proper conditions. |

Methods may be stable, strained, degraded, corrupted, mutated, obsolete, externalized, or source-local.

## Method creation threshold

A Method Record is created only when a pattern is repeated, trained, proof-bearing, teachable, risky, source-local, owner-handoff relevant, or mechanically distinct enough to matter beyond a single scene. Not every habit becomes a method.

## Method vs Technique vs Expression

A Method remains D05-owned when it is a practiced application of competency with ordinary or bounded effect. A Technique begins D06 handoff when it becomes a formal move, maneuver, art, route structure, or defined capability with progression implications. An Expression begins D03/D06 handoff when it becomes retained or activated power-capability with cost, carrier, burden, Power Economy use, inscription, or ascendant effect.

## Examples

Hammer Rhythm as D05 Method helps reduce cracking when forging difficult metal. As D06 Technique candidate it becomes formal forge art. As D03 Expression candidate it uses Zi, Mistra, Scrypta, Pressa, Calora, or another economy to alter metal beyond ordinary craft.

Depth Reading as D05 Method lets a Fisher interpret hidden motion, hazards, prey behavior, or unnatural currents. D06 becomes involved if it becomes a route action; D03 if it becomes powered by Tellura/Sympathis/Zi; D04 if it supports Far Fisher proof.

Ash-Scent Lure as D05 Method uses smoke, ash, fat, flour, hunger, and pack behavior to influence mundane pack movement. D08 becomes involved if creature influence/control begins; D06 if it becomes compulsion or route method; D03 if powered; D04 if tied to Wolfbane proof.

## Method formalization triggers

Repetition, pressure, teaching, research, specialization, proof, tool/anchor dependency, breakthrough, route pressure, Expression pressure, and source-local pressure can formalize methods.

## Mastery benefits

Mastery may produce reliability, relevance upgrade, cost efficiency, consequence control, information depth, method creation, specialization access, teaching authority, research acceleration, proof weight, and route pressure. It does not automatically grant free Expressions, unrestricted action declarations, automatic Path/Class/Dao identity, tier bypass, capstone effects, or universal advantage.

## Teaching authority

Teaching authority begins at high competency and improves through mastery, proof, method formalization, and transmission experience. It may require sufficient rank, communication ability, compatible learner, time, tools/environment, method clarity, proof of use, and source-local access. Not everyone who knows a competency can teach it well.

## Method record doctrine shape

A future method record should preserve method name, parent competencies, state, use case, conditions/tools, effect summary, limits, risks, relevance profile, proof/training/research links, handoff candidates, visibility state, and validation state. This is doctrine shape, not final schema.
