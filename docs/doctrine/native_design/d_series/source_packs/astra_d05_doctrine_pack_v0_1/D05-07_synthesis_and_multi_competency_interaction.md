# D05-07 Synthesis and Multi-Competency Interaction

**Astra Ascension Doctrine Pack:** D05 — Competency, Profession, Knowledge, Training, Research, and Synthesis  
**Version:** v0.1  
**Generated:** 2026-05-31  
**Status:** Design doctrine generated from accepted D05 decisions.  
**Scope rule:** D05 defines capability substrate. It does not authorize final Expression mechanics, Power Economy math, Path/Class/Dao identity, breakthrough outcomes, item evolution, companion evolution, faction authority, territory control, canon sourcebook text, runtime database schema, or live-play GM behavior.

## Design question

How should Astra handle multiple competencies interacting in one action, project, method, research track, profession pattern, or route-pressure event?

## Core synthesis law

Synthesis is a structured interaction between multiple capability sources. It does not stack roll bonuses. One source provides primary roll posture; other sources may provide secondary effects. Durable synthesis requires evidence, repetition, practice, research, proof, meaningful use, or validation. Synthesis may create methods, specializations, research tracks, proof clusters, teaching forms, action vectors, route pressure, Expression or Technique candidates, harmful states, source-local states, or owner-file handoffs. Any unusual synthesis is routed by function and owner-file impact rather than rejected for not appearing on a predefined list.

## Synthesis is not stacking

D05 restricts primary roll posture to one source. Synthesis is about structured outputs, not bonus accumulation. It should not become hidden stacking.

## Interaction types

- **Support synthesis:** multiple competencies support one action without durable method.
- **Interpretive synthesis:** competencies combine to understand something none could interpret alone.
- **Method synthesis:** repeated interaction creates a D05 Method.
- **Profession synthesis:** a profession absorbs knowledge/proof/method from another life-pattern.
- **Research synthesis:** competencies make a Research Track possible.
- **Tool synthesis:** competency becomes bound to tool, relic, platform, workshop, AI, or anchor.
- **Teaching synthesis:** mastered competency plus method becomes teachable.
- **Route-pressure synthesis:** competencies plus proof point toward route.
- **Expression-candidate synthesis:** competencies and methods plus Power Economy access create Expression candidate.
- **Harmful synthesis:** false analogies, incompatible assumptions, or unstable interaction create risk.

## States

Incidental, Emerging, Recorded, Practiced, Formalized, Teachable, Externalized, Unstable, Harmful, Source-local, Escalated.

## Durability gate

A synthesis becomes durable only when supported by functional interaction, evidence, repetition, practice, research, proof, meaningful use, pressure, teaching, or owner-file validation. A one-time clever description may create incidental support but not automatically create a Synthesis Record.

## Functional relationship test

Before recording synthesis, ask what components are interacting, whether the relationship is functional or decorative, what new output exists, whether it stays in D05, whether owner handoff is needed, whether it is stable/unstable/harmful/source-local/escalated, and what state delta should be recorded.

## Valid D05 outputs

Secondary relevance, improved information yield, reduced consequence, training progress, research question, research track, method seed, practiced method, specialization seed, proof record, profession signature, teaching form, route pressure, action vector, source-local note, owner-file handoff.

## Invalid final outputs without owner validation

Expression, Technique, Path/Class/Dao, Power Economy change, tier authorization, capstone transformation, item growth, companion growth, faction rank, territory authority, corruption purification.

## Anti-abuse rule

Synthesis is not automatic because two competencies sound related. Blacksmithing + Swordsmanship may synthesize around blade balance, maintenance, edge behavior, or anti-armor design. It does not automatically grant superior combat damage in every fight. Cooking + Alchemy may synthesize around extraction, fermentation, dosage, and heat control. It does not automatically create every potion recipe.

## Any-eventuality routing

For unusual synthesis, classify by function. What components interact? What changes? What output is proposed? Which owner file would break if mishandled? Does it create proof, training, research, method, specialization, or route pressure? Does it touch energy, route, harm, actor, item, faction, territory, world-state, or source-local rules? Which owner validates?

## Examples

Sword relic: Blacksmithing primary; Metallurgy and Relic Appraisal secondary; Hammer Rhythm interprets forged stress. Outputs may include interpretive synthesis, research question, proof, and D09 handoff.

Wisp of Steel: Earthen Wisp knowledge + Mistra + Pressa + Metallurgy creates research synthesis. D03 validates cost, partition, burden, confluence. D06 validates route technique if relevant.

Baker-wolf-slayer: Baking + smoke/ash + wolf proof + survival creates profession synthesis, Ash-Scent Lure method seed, proof cluster, route pressure, and title seed. D04/D06/D08/D03 handoffs may apply.

Pilot and AI platform: Platform Operation + AI Interface + Tactical Insight + Neural Link creates tool/platform synthesis. D09/D08 handoff; harmful synthesis possible if assumptions mismatch.

## Synthesis record doctrine shape

A Synthesis Record should preserve name, actor, state, components, interaction type, functional relationship, primary effect, secondary effects, limits, risks, proof/training/research outputs, owner handoffs, and validation state. Doctrine shape only; final schema deferred.
