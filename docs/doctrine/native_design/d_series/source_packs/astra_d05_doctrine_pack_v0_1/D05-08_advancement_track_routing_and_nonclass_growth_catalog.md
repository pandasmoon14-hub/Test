# D05-08 Advancement Track Routing and Nonclass Growth Catalog

**Astra Ascension Doctrine Pack:** D05 — Competency, Profession, Knowledge, Training, Research, and Synthesis  
**Version:** v0.1  
**Generated:** 2026-05-31  
**Status:** Design doctrine generated from accepted D05 decisions.  
**Scope rule:** D05 defines capability substrate. It does not authorize final Expression mechanics, Power Economy math, Path/Class/Dao identity, breakthrough outcomes, item evolution, companion evolution, faction authority, territory control, canon sourcebook text, runtime database schema, or live-play GM behavior.

## Design question

How should Astra handle many advancement tracks separate from level, tier, class, Path/Class/Dao, and ordinary ability acquisition?

## Routing catalog, not canon catalog

This file does not promote the advancement-track pressure inventory into canon. It defines how Astra recognizes, routes, contains, records, or escalates advancement tracks that appear through design, conversion, runtime play, or player-created development.

The large advancement-track list is a non-exhaustive pressure inventory. It is not final terminology, not a fixed mechanics catalog, and not a D05-owned system list.

## Core Advancement Track law

Astra recognizes Advancement Tracks as durable growth lanes, but every track must have lawful ownership. D05 owns competency-based tracks and routes non-D05 tracks by function to the proper doctrine file. The advancement-track pressure inventory is non-exhaustive and non-canonical. Development Picks, proof interaction, unlocks, and mechanical effects require owner-file validation. Donor-shaped terminology remains contained unless formally promoted.

## Definition

An Advancement Track is any durable growth lane that records progress, rank, depth, state, access, authorization, transformation, or accumulation. It may measure proficiency, mastery, knowledge, training, research, proof, title, reputation, resource capacity, energy refinement, mutation, corruption, companion bond, item refinement, territory development, faction standing, domain authority, settlement growth, route pressure, concept comprehension, platform synchronization, or actor-state transformation.

It is not automatically a level, tier, class, Path/Class/Dao, Expression, or canon mechanic.

## Source-local tracks

Some tracks are valid only in bounded context: donor-specific cultivation organs, local divine ranks, campaign-only reputation currency, unique relic ladders, faction initiation counters, setting-local cosmology progressions, adventure-path achievement chains. A source-local track may be preserved, converted, or referenced only inside its boundary unless later canon review promotes a stripped Astra-compatible function.

## Function-based routing test

Ask: what is progressing; what changes when it progresses; what it unlocks or authorizes; what state must be tracked; what system would break if mishandled; whether it involves learned capability, energy/resource/cost/carrier, proof/level/tier/title/breakthrough, route/technique/domain/concept, harm/corruption/recovery/mutation, actor/companion/bond/summon/AI, item/relic/tool/platform, faction/territory/world-state, or source-local rules; and which owner file must validate it.

Surface theme does not decide ownership.

## Proof relation categories

Tracks can generate proof, require proof, bind proof, transform proof, reference proof, or be not proof-bearing. D04 owns proof sufficiency.

## Development Pick interaction

Development Picks may target Advancement Tracks only when owner file validates the spend. A visible track is not automatically a legal pick target.

## Track anti-sprawl rule

Do not create durable tracks for every minor habit, temporary buff, item bonus, short-term condition, single event, or flavor label. A track becomes durable only when it has rank/depth progression, repeatable improvement, proof relation, unlocks, state change, owner-file handoff, visibility state, long-term relevance, conversion need, runtime tracking need, or source-local boundary requirement.

## Donor-term containment

Terms such as dantian, meridian, tribulation, Dao, divine spark, godhood, universal law, karma points, fate points, and similar are signals, not canon. They do not become default Astra terms unless formally adopted. Meridian Channels may become D03/D06 carrier/channel progression. Dao Comprehension may become D06 concept/domain/route authority. Tribulation Counter may become D04 ordeal/breakthrough proof. Godhood Potential may become capstone/Ascension/source-local pressure. Karma Score may route to D10/D04 and must not be confused with hidden Pneuma.

## Generic track doctrine shape

Track records should preserve track id, actor/entity, name, family, owner file, visibility state, progress state, rank/depth, proof refs, training/research/method/synthesis refs, source-local boundary, outputs/unlocks, validation state, and handoff refs. This is doctrine shape, not final schema.

## UI grouping

Future UI should group tracks by Skills & Knowledge, Professions, Training & Research, Power & Expressions, Advancement, Routes, Conditions & Change, Bonds & Actors, Gear & Platforms, World & Standing, and Local/Special. Final UI remains deferred.
