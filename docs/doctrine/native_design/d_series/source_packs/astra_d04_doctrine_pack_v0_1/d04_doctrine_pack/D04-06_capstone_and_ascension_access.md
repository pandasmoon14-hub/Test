# D04-06 — Capstone and Ascension Access

Status: `doctrine-draft / accepted D04 design decisions`  
Layer: D04 advancement doctrine  
Owner: capstone architecture and reserved Ascension Access  
Depends on: D04 Proof Bundles, Tier Authorization, Breakthrough Procedure, D07/D08/D10 later for final consequences

## 1. Purpose

This file defines levels 95–100, Tier 10, Capstone Picks, Capstone Proof Ladders, final-form advancement, and the reserved Level 100 Ascension Access rule.

Capstone is not ordinary high-level advancement. It is the final proof of what the character has become and what consequences their existence creates.

## 2. Capstone principle

Levels 95–100 are not ordinary advancement. They are final-proof levels where the actor resolves what they have become, what scale they can affect, what cost they can bear, and what the world must now acknowledge, resist, record, or be changed by.

## 3. Capstone Band Procedure

D04 uses the Capstone Band Procedure for levels 95–100.

| Level | Function |
|---:|---|
| 95 | Capstone Threshold Entry |
| 96 | Capstone Axis Consolidation |
| 97 | Capstone Contradiction / Cost Confrontation |
| 98 | Capstone World / Domain / Recognition Proof |
| 99 | Final Integration / Irreversible Choice |
| 100 | Final Capstone Resolution |

These are functional roles, not forced story beats.

## 4. Level 95 — Capstone Threshold Entry

Level 95 is the gate from Tier 9 into Tier 10.

Requires Capstone Proof Bundle eligibility; Mythic or Capstone sufficiency band; unresolved route contradictions surfaced; at least one world/domain/faction/territory/actor-scale consequence; defined capstone direction; and warning that progression begins final-form pressure.

Functional warning:

```text
Progressing into the capstone band will begin resolving the character's highest route, deepest contradiction, greatest proof, and most severe consequence. Delay remains possible, but capstone pressure may alter route options.
```

## 5. Level 96 — Capstone Axis Consolidation

The actor identifies and binds the capstone-relevant axes. This does not mean every axis is maximized. It means D04 records which parts of the actor's life matter to final form.

Possible capstone axes include Path/Class/Dao route, primary Power Economy, key Expression family, title/achievement chain, domain/affinity/concept, territory/faction/world-state, relic/tool/platform/bond, profession-transmutation, body/Animus/Integrity transformation, corruption/purification axis, and codex/knowledge/exploration axis.

## 6. Level 97 — Capstone Contradiction / Cost Confrontation

The actor confronts a major contradiction, debt, scar, corruption, unresolved route conflict, or cost.

Examples include a healer choosing what cannot be saved; a worldbreaker facing what survives them; a corrupted ascendant purifying, accepting, quarantining, or becoming corruption; a relic-bound wanderer deciding whether the relic is tool, prison, companion, or self; a ruler paying for the structure they built; or a crafter facing independent consequence of their creations.

Raw proof cannot skip this function. It requires resolution, integration, severance, sacrifice, transformation, or equivalent final handling.

## 7. Level 98 — Capstone World / Domain / Recognition Proof

Final advancement is no longer private. Something outside the actor must respond.

Valid forms include faction recognition, world-state change, domain response, territory transformation, ecological response, myth-recorded deed, hidden cosmic notice, planar pressure, relic awakening, companion/bond transformation, enemy/faction opposition, source-local recognition, community consequence, world repair, world damage, or absence recognized by hidden systems.

Recognition is not always fame.

## 8. Level 99 — Final Integration / Irreversible Choice

The actor must integrate or commit.

Possible outcomes include route fusion, route severance, corruption acceptance, corruption purification, final oath, title binding, final Expression re-authoring, Power Economy reconstitution, actor-state transition, externalization into relic/domain/platform, sacrifice of an axis, binding to territory/faction/world, or final whole-character breakthrough preparation.

This is the last level before final resolution. It defines what cannot remain undecided.

## 9. Level 100 — Final Capstone Resolution

Level 100 resolves the capstone arc. It usually requires whole-character breakthrough, final Path/Class/Dao transformation, final-form route stabilization, mythic title embodiment, world/plane/domain pressure resolution, actor-state transformation, final Expression or economy culmination, final sacrifice or integration, or source-local endgame transformation.

Level 100 may produce Capstone Payload, final-form route, actor-state change, permanent world-state consequence, capstone title, final scar, final external anchor, ascendant legacy, irreversible severance, endgame playable state, retirement, transformation, rulership, dissolution, continued play, or Ascension Access.

D04 does not decide that level 100 ends play. It allows both continued capstone play and campaign-ending resolution.

## 10. Capstone Proof Ladders

Capstone requires multiple proof lanes.

| Proof lane | Function |
|---|---|
| Identity Proof | What the actor has become, accepted, rejected, integrated, or severed. |
| Mastery Proof | What axis, route, Expression, economy, title, bond, territory, or domain reached defining depth. |
| Cost Proof | What price was paid or consciously accepted. |
| Consequence Proof | What changed because of the actor. |
| Recognition Proof | Who or what acknowledges, resists, records, reacts, remembers, or is altered. |
| Structural Proof | What changed in Vessel, Animus, Integrity, carrier, route, economy, actor state, or external anchor. |
| Capstone Route Proof | Why this final form is lawful for this specific actor. |

Not every capstone uses every lane identically, but capstone cannot rest on one ordinary proof type.

## 11. Capstone Picks

Capstone Picks replace ordinary Development Picks during levels 95–100.

A Capstone Pick may target whole-character breakthrough, final Path/Class/Dao transformation, capstone Expression, Power Economy reconstitution, route fusion/severance, mythic title embodiment, domain authority, world/faction/territory culmination, relic/tool/platform/companion finalization, actor-state transformation, final scar integration, final corruption/purification decision, legacy/continuation structure, or Ascension Access preparation.

Each Capstone Pick requires Capstone Proof, Tier 10 authorization, owner-file validation, state-delta recording, explicit cost/consequence review, and capstone warning if irreversible.

Capstone Picks should not be hoarded casually. Held Capstone Picks become Capstone Pressure rather than ordinary held picks.

## 12. Capstone route variants

| Route variant | Emphasis |
|---|---|
| Mastery Capstone | Final mastery of route, Expression, economy, profession, domain. |
| Sacrifice Capstone | Power is earned through irreversible cost. |
| Integration Capstone | Contradictions, scars, routes, economies reconciled. |
| Severance Capstone | Final form comes from cutting away route, debt, corruption, identity. |
| Fusion Capstone | Multiple routes become coherent final structure. |
| Corruption Capstone | Actor accepts, controls, becomes, or transforms through corruption. |
| Purification Capstone | Actor cleanses, redeems, removes, or transforms hostile pressure. |
| World Capstone | Territory, faction, domain, planet, plane, or world-state becomes central. |
| Relic / Externalized Capstone | Final form depends on external anchor, object, platform, companion, place. |
| Mythic Title Capstone | Title/legend becomes mechanically and metaphysically real. |
| Profession Capstone | Mundane-origin profession reaches impossible ascendant finality. |
| Hidden / Source-local Capstone | Campaign-specific mechanism remains bounded. |

## 13. Capstone outcomes

Capstone does not have to mean becoming a god.

Possible outcomes include continued capstone play, final ascendant form, rulership/domain binding, world repair, world damage, self-sacrifice, transformation into spirit/relic/domain/myth/faction anchor/world-force, retirement as living legend, playable altered actor state, final severance from prior route, corruption acceptance, purification, impossible profession embodiment, source-local final state, or Ascension Access.

The outcome must be earned by proof and recorded as state change.

## 14. Catastrophic capstone failure

Capstone attempts can fail. Failure may cause route collapse, actor-state fracture, world-state backlash, mythic title inversion, corruption outbreak, external anchor destruction, faction/domain retaliation, permanent route loss, lethal breakthrough, unmaking, or unintended transformation.

Catastrophic outcomes require lethal-risk posture, catastrophic posture, source-local procedure, or strong pressure trail.

## 15. Ascension Access rule

At level 100, after Final Capstone Resolution, an entity may gain Ascension Access if its Capstone Proof, route state, actor state, and unresolved consequences support it.

Ascension Access creates the opportunity to attempt, delay, refuse, suppress, externalize, redirect, or transform Ascension. It does not force immediate departure from play.

## 16. Capstone versus Ascension

Capstone is the final proof of what the actor has become inside the current advancement frame.

Ascension is the opportunity to cross beyond that frame into a higher-order state, scale, realm, reality-density, world-role, actor-state, or post-capstone existence.

An entity can reach level 100 and refuse Ascension. An entity can remain level 100 to guard a world, complete unfinished business, stabilize territory, build faction, protect descendants, prepare further, or pursue alternate endgame state.

## 17. Reserved Ascension postures

| Posture | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Attempt Ascension | Actor chooses to cross. |
| Delay Ascension | Actor waits after gaining access. |
| Suppress Ascension | Actor intentionally holds back crossing pressure. |
| Refuse Ascension | Actor rejects the offered transition. |
| Externalize Ascension | Actor attempts through relic, platform, territory, faction, companion, domain. |
| Redirect Ascension | Actor routes pressure into worldbinding, profession finality, purification, or other final state. |
| Failed Ascension | Attempt fails and produces harm, regression, exile, scar, altered state. |
| Partial Ascension | Actor gains higher-order quality but remains limited. |
| Worldbound State | Actor binds to current world, territory, people, law, domain, or duty. |
| Corrupted Ascension | Actor crosses through Vitia, hostile force, parasitic route, unstable transformation. |
| Purified Ascension | Actor ascends by cleansing, reconciling, or transforming corruption/debt. |
| Loose / Incomplete Ascendant | Actor survives failed/refused crossing and becomes powerful but unstable, hunted, pressured, or periodically tested. |
| Source-local Ascension | Campaign/conversion-specific ascension mechanism remains bounded. |

## 18. Profession Ascension

D04 explicitly preserves noncombat capstone and Ascension Access.

A crafter, healer, farmer, cook, navigator, teacher, ruler, scribe, architect, beast-bonder, merchant, settlement-founder, scholar, alchemist-like figure, blacksmith, or other noncombatant can reach capstone and Ascension Access if proof reaches capstone sufficiency.

Profession Ascension may mean craft becomes higher-order principle; service becomes world-recognized law; healing becomes reality repair; governance becomes domain binding; farming becomes ecological world-shaping; cooking becomes life-pattern transformation; scholarship becomes living archive; or trade becomes causal/economic world-thread authority.

## 19. What D04 reserves but does not solve

D04 reserves Ascension Access as a level 100 opportunity.

D04 does not define exact Ascension trial mechanics, higher-realm cosmology, post-Ascension power reset math, higher-density reality rules, social demotion mechanics, return restrictions, immortal body mechanics, post-Ascension economy, full worldbound/loose ascendant procedure, or final post-capstone play loop.

These belong to D08/D10/D03/D06/D07/D09 and later canon/sourcebook/runtime design.

## 20. Source-local ascension containment

Donor ascension, apotheosis, immortal realm transition, divine transformation, loose immortal, world guardian, realm migration, final trial, epic destiny, or profession ascension mechanics can map into Ascension Access categories.

Donor higher-realm cosmology, tribulation names, energy names, metaphysical assumptions, and exact progression ranks remain source-local unless deliberately promoted.

## 21. Validation rules

A capstone procedure is invalid if it treats levels 95–99 as ordinary levels; gives only level 100 special handling; uses ordinary Development Picks instead of Capstone Picks; requires one prescribed capstone route; blocks noncombat capstone paths; forces immediate Ascension at level 100; treats Tier 10 as merely stronger Tier 9; causes catastrophic failure without lethal-risk posture or pressure trail; imports donor ascension cosmology as Astra default; or erases continued capstone play.
