## Architecture

D14 uses Posture–Route–Discovery Architecture. It supports urban, wilderness, site/interior, space/platform, dimensional/realm, hazardous/anomalous, pursuit/evasion, tactical, horror, oracle, and mixed exploration without adopting any one donor model.

## Donor-family pressures

D14 must withstand d20 travel pace and passive perception, OSR dungeon turns and wandering monsters, hexcrawl route and weather procedures, sci-fi ship navigation and fuel assumptions, realm/cultivation threshold travel, horror hidden-site discovery, narrative player-authored map facts, cyberpunk surveillance and restricted districts, tactical map/terrain assumptions, and random-table/oracle exploration.

## Embedded risk controls

D14 must not universalize hexes, grids, zones, nodes, dungeon turns, watches, travel days, random encounters, scouting checks, room reveal text, ship travel law, realm metaphysics, supply attrition, or map truth. Unsupported procedure must route to source-local retention, quarantine, or escalation.
