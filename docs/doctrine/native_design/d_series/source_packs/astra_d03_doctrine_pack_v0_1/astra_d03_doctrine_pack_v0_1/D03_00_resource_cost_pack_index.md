## 1. Purpose

This file indexes the D03 doctrine pack for Astra Ascension.

D03 owns the architecture of power economies, costs, reservoirs, expression retention, expression validation, overdraw, backlash, corruption, and recovery. D03 does not finalize exact numerical formulas, final balance, full harm procedure, full advancement math, final path/domain access, or runtime implementation.

D03 exists because Astra does not use a single mana pool, a fixed spell slot system, or a donor-shaped resource model. Astra uses a modular power-economy lattice capable of supporting mundane embodiment, trained power systems, custom player-created Expressions, converted donor abilities, external anchors, relics, overlimit experimentation, and long-term character transformation.

## 3. Accepted D03 principles

### D03-P0 — Power Economy Lattice

Astra uses a Power Economy Lattice, not one pool and not seven fixed attribute pools. Each Power Economy has source logic, storage mode, spend pattern, recovery pattern, risks, failure modes, interaction behavior, and availability.

### D03-P1 — No antagonist-only economy category

No Power Economy is structurally antagonist-only. Economies may be restricted, rare, dangerous, source-local, hidden, GM-facing, or threat-facing, but not inherently antagonist-only.

### D03-P2 — No standalone temporal economy

The prior temporal/chrono economy is removed as a standalone D03 Power Economy. Temporal effects may exist as rare effects, source-local phenomena, relic behavior, Karmax causal pressure, Sortis probability drift, Aethra realm manipulation, Mnemora memory patterning, Scrypta encoded delay, or other routed expressions.

### D03-P3 — Endura and Health baseline

All physically embodied entities have Health/Vital State and Endura as baseline embodiment systems. Health is a harm/survival state primarily owned downstream by D07. Endura is baseline stamina/exertion for embodied action, travel, labor, overexertion, fatigue, and mundane physical pressure. Neither Health nor Endura counts against the three integrated Power Economy tolerance.

### D03-P4 — Soft three-economy Vessel limit

A standard Vessel can stably integrate up to three non-baseline Power Economies. This is a soft tolerance limit, not a hard metaphysical impossibility. Exceeding it creates overlimit pressure unless stabilized, externalized, transformed, or supported by doctrine-defined means.

### D03-P5 — Shared Power Reservoir

A character gains a shared Power Reservoir upon integrating a first non-baseline Power Economy. Additional integrated economies add fractional capacity rather than full separate pools. The reservoir is partitioned by integrated economy.

### D03-P6 — Dominant / Secondary / Tertiary default

The first integrated economy is Dominant by default, the second is Secondary, and the third is Tertiary. Advanced customization may later allow different allocations or reordering through meaningful procedures.

### D03-P7 — Expression is neutral doctrine term

Expression is the doctrine-neutral term for a functional power/effect pattern. Local terms such as spell, technique, rite, art, formula, working, maneuver, protocol, craft, dao-working, prayer, or discipline may be used by culture, path, faction, source-local content, or player-facing presentation.

### D03-P8 — Governed authoring and re-authoring

Converted, canonical, and player-created Expressions use the same validation grammar. Re-authoring an Expression through a new economy requires compatibility, route, cost, risk, bandwidth, and at least one mechanical vector change.

### D03-P9 — Desired Outcome is not an attempted Expression

A player may describe any desired outcome. A character may attempt an Expression only when a lawful route exists. Without a lawful route, the desired outcome becomes theory, research, training, project, quest pressure, or experimentation vector rather than a supernatural action.

### D03-P10 — Expression capacity is burden/tolerance based

Characters can know, observe, theorize, study, copy, or learn more Expressions than they can retain. Retaining Expressions depends on Expression Burden and Carrier Tolerance, not flat donor-style slots. Expressions are not freely swapped like equipment.

## 4. D03 boundaries

### D03 owns

- Power Economy definition and interaction grammar.
- Endura as baseline embodied exertion economy.
- Non-baseline Power Reservoir unlock logic.
- Reservoir partition roles and shift permissions.
- Expression cost and route architecture.
- Expression authoring/re-authoring validation hooks.
- Expression burden, carrier tolerance, inscription condition, and retention pressure.
- Overdraw, overlimit pressure, instability, backlash, corruption/Vitia interface, and recovery categories.
- Runtime/UI state requirements for D03-owned concepts.

### D03 does not own

- D02 roll procedure, d20 margins, critical lanes, or final action resolution.
- D04 level/tier/breakthrough formulas.
- D05 exact skill, profession, knowledge, or competency taxonomy.
- D06 path/domain/archetype/profession/technique access rules.
- D07 final harm, injury, death-risk, resistance, or combat damage math.
- D08 complete actor taxonomy for nonphysical entities, companions, summons, AIs, spirits, and platforms.
- D09 full item/relic/tool/implant/vehicle/platform schema.
- D10 faction, travel, law, economy, and world-scale procedure.
- Final runtime implementation or UI code.
- Final canon promotion or sourcebook prose.

## 5. Deferred calibration

The following are deliberately deferred:

- exact reservoir formulas;
- exact secondary/tertiary fractional capacity values;
- exact partition percentages;
- exact Expression Grade budgets;
- exact cost floors by grade;
- exact burden formula;
- exact carrier tolerance formula;
- exact recovery rates;
- exact overdraw thresholds;
- exact backlash tables;
- exact corruption/Vitia progression;
- final lexicon acceptance of economy names;
- final UI layout.

D03 Phase 1 closes the design spine. D03 Phase 2 can calibrate values after D04–D07 provide advancement, skill, access, and harm structures.

---

## 6. Conversion posture

Donor resource systems must not be imported directly. During conversion, resource constructs should be decomposed into:

- Power Economy route;
- storage mode;
- spend pattern;
- recovery pattern;
- risk/failure mode;
- Expression route;
- retention/inscription requirement;
- external carrier or internal integration;
- source-local boundary;
- exact donor math as evidence only.

Donor terms such as mana, qi, chakra, spell slot, stamina, heat, karma, faith, psionics, blood magic, stress, corruption, and similar constructs are translation inputs, not Astra law.

## 7. Closing condition

D03 is considered architecturally closed when D03-00 through D03-08 exist and preserve all accepted decisions above, with exact numerical calibration deferred.
