# Astra Ascension — D04/D05 Reconciliation Amendment

Version: v0.1  
Status: proposed amendment / required before D06  
Applies to: D04 Advancement, Proof, Awakening, Development Pick, Tier, Breakthrough, Capstone, and Advancement Axis Doctrine  
Source pressure: D05 Competency, Profession, Knowledge, Training, Research, Synthesis, and Advancement Track Doctrine

---

## 1. Purpose

D05 established that competencies, professions, knowledge fields, training, research, methods, mastery, specialization, synthesis, teaching, and advancement tracks can all produce proof, route pressure, action vectors, and owner-file handoffs. D04 must now recognize these as advancement inputs without letting D05 decide proof sufficiency, level advancement, Awakening, tier transition, breakthrough, capstone, or Ascension Access.

This amendment integrates D05 into D04 while preserving D04 ownership.

---

D05 does not decide proof sufficiency or advancement authorization.

## 3. Core D04/D05 interface laws

### D04/D05-L1 — D05 records can generate proof, but D04 weighs proof

D05 records may become proof inputs when they involve meaningful action, training, research, mastery, method formation, synthesis, teaching, route pressure, or profession-shaping events.

D04 decides:

- whether the record is proof-bearing;
- proof family;
- proof weight;
- contextual proof weight;
- whether it binds, transforms, references, or merely records proof;
- whether it satisfies a gate.

### D04/D05-L2 — Competency proof is not automatically advancement proof

A competency rank increase, training project, or research track is not automatically proof for level, tier, breakthrough, Awakening, or capstone.

It becomes D04-relevant when it changes capability, identity, route pressure, cost, consequence, world-state, risk, mastery, or meaningful direction.

### D04/D05-L3 — Professions generate Awakening pressure, not automatic routes

A Profession Record may feed Awakening Proof, Dynamic Triadic Choice, route pressure, title seeds, Development Pick targets, and capstone proof.

It does not automatically become Path/Class/Dao. D04 evaluates proof and D06 formalizes route identity.

### D04/D05-L4 — D05 Advancement Tracks route into D04 but do not replace D04 axes

D05-08 defines Advancement Tracks as general growth lanes. D04 should use this routing grammar to refine D04-04 Advancement Axis Registry.

D04 owns proof-facing axis handling, layer grants, tier authorization, and advancement gates. D05 owns competency-based tracks and routes other tracks to their owners.

### D04/D05-L5 — Development Picks require owner validation

A Development Pick may target a D05 track only if the target has a lawful D05 form and D04 validates the spend.

Examples:

- improve a Training Project;
- unlock a specialization;
- formalize a method;
- bind proof to a profession track;
- preserve a research-derived action vector;
- stabilize route pressure for later D06 review.

A visible track is not automatically a legal Development Pick target.

### D04/D05-L6 — Breakthroughs may use D05 basis but remain D04-owned

D05 can supply:

- lawful attempt vector;
- training support;
- research basis;
- method seed;
- synthesis record;
- profession proof;
- mastery proof;
- harmful synthesis or dangerous research risk.

D04 still decides breakthrough eligibility, risk posture, response menu, resolution mode, payload, failure consequences, and state deltas.

### D04/D05-L7 — Capstone must admit D05 proof diversity

Capstone proof must explicitly support:

- profession mastery;
- teaching lineage;
- research culmination;
- synthesis formalization;
- knowledge completion;
- method embodiment;
- noncombat mastery;
- impossible craft/service/labor routes;
- D05-owned Advancement Tracks.

This preserves noncombatant and mundane-origin capstones.

## DDR Fragment — D04/D05 Reconciliation

Status: proposed amendment

Design question:
- How should D04 integrate D05 competency, profession, training, research, method, synthesis, and advancement-track doctrine without surrendering proof and advancement ownership?

Current working answer:
- D05 records can generate proof inputs, route pressure, Awakening vectors, Development Pick targets, breakthrough basis, capstone proof, and advancement-track routing. D04 evaluates proof weight, sufficiency, advancement gate eligibility, Awakening, tier transition, breakthrough, capstone, and Ascension Access. D05 supports advancement but does not authorize it.

Astra-native principle:
- Practiced life, study, labor, method, and synthesis can become advancement evidence, but advancement remains proof-governed and owner-routed.

Mechanical implication:
- Add D05 proof-source families.
- Add D05 Awakening inputs.
- Add D05 Development Pick targets.
- Route D04 Advancement Axis Registry through D05-08.
- Add D05 basis to breakthrough rules.
- Add D05 capstone proof forms.
- Add D05 runtime refs.

Player-facing implication:
- Professions, research, mastery, teaching, and synthesis can matter in advancement without becoming automatic classes or powers.

Conversion implication:
- Donor profession growth, skill mastery, research systems, title chains, crafting achievements, and noncombat progression can map into proof and advancement tracks without donor-law import.

Runtime implication:
- Backend must connect D05 records to proof, pick, Awakening, breakthrough, capstone, and axis systems while preserving owner validation.

Affected files:
- D04-00 through D04-08.

Decision needed:
- Accept and apply this D04 amendment before D06 begins.
