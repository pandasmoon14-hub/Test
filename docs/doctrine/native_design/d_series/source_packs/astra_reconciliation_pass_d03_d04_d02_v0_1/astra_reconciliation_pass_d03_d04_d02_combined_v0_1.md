# Astra Ascension — D03/D04/D02 Reconciliation Pass Manifest

Version: v0.1  
Status: amendment pack / pre-D06 gate  
Scope: D05-to-D03, D05-to-D04, and optional D02 relevance cross-check

## Purpose

This pack performs the required reconciliation after completion of D05: Competency, Profession, Knowledge, Training, Research, Synthesis, and Advancement Track doctrine.

It does not rewrite D03, D04, or D02 wholesale. It creates controlled amendment files that can be applied to the existing doctrine packs or used as patch instructions before beginning D06.

## Files

1. `D03_D05_reconciliation_amendment_v0_1.md`  
   Integrates D05 training, research, synthesis, method, competency, and advancement-track logic into D03 Power Economy and Expression doctrine.

2. `D04_D05_reconciliation_amendment_v0_1.md`  
   Integrates D05 competencies, professions, research, mastery, synthesis, and advancement-track routing into D04 proof, Development Picks, Awakening, tier benefits, breakthrough, and capstone doctrine.

3. `D02_D05_relevance_crosscheck_v0_1.md`  
   Adds an optional but recommended D02 cross-check for D05 relevance handling, lawful action vectors, check support, cost/consequence effects, and d20 roll-posture boundaries.

4. `reconciliation_manifest.json`  
   Machine-readable manifest for the reconciliation pass.

## Reconciliation principle

D05 is now the capability-substrate layer. It can record, support, route, and generate candidates. It does not authorize final Expression mechanics, Power Economy changes, breakthrough outcomes, Path/Class/Dao identity, item growth, companion growth, faction authority, territory state, or world-state changes.

D03, D04, and D02 must therefore be amended to reference D05 without surrendering their ownership.

## Required sequencing

Before D06 begins:

1. Apply or accept the D03 reconciliation amendment.
2. Apply or accept the D04 reconciliation amendment.
3. Review the optional D02 relevance cross-check.
4. Confirm that D06 may begin with a clean distinction between competency, profession, method, route pressure, Technique, Expression, and Path/Class/Dao.

## Non-ownership note

This reconciliation pack is not canon sourcebook prose, not final runtime schema, not live-play GM behavior, and not training/evaluation example material. It is design doctrine and patch guidance.

# Astra Ascension — D03/D05 Reconciliation Amendment

Version: v0.1  
Status: proposed amendment / required before D06  
Applies to: D03 Resource, Power Economy, Expression, Carrier, Overdraw, and Runtime State Doctrine  
Source pressure: D05 Competency, Profession, Knowledge, Training, Research, Synthesis, and Advancement Track Doctrine

---

## 1. Purpose

D05 introduced competency, profession, training, research, mastery, method, synthesis, relevance, records, and advancement-track routing doctrine. D03 must now acknowledge how those structures feed Power Economy and Expression systems without allowing D05 to authorize final D03 mechanics.

This amendment does not redesign D03. It adds controlled interfaces.

The core reconciliation question is:

How do D05 competencies, training projects, research tracks, methods, synthesis records, and advancement tracks support D03 Power Economy and Expression development without bypassing D03 validation?

---

## 2. Amendment status

D03 remains the owner of:

- Power Economy access, integration, and compatibility;
- reservoir partitioning;
- carrier tolerance;
- Expression authoring and re-authoring;
- Expression validation;
- Expression inscription and burden;
- overdraw, backlash, instability, and resource-facing corruption;
- D03 runtime state requirements.

D05 now becomes an input layer for:

- lawful action-vector creation;
- current basis and missing-basis identification;
- training toward Power Economy control;
- research toward Expression candidates;
- method seeds that may become Expressions or Techniques;
- synthesis records that may feed re-authoring or confluence;
- competency relevance affecting cost, stability, risk, information yield, and validation posture.

D05 does not directly change Power Economy, Expression, carrier, reservoir, cost, burden, or overdraw state. D03 validates those changes.

---

## 3. Core D03/D05 interface laws

### D03/D05-L1 — D05 creates basis, not final authorization

A D05 record may establish why an actor has a lawful basis to attempt, research, train toward, or prototype a D03-facing outcome.

D05 may produce:

- Research Track;
- Training Project;
- Method Seed;
- Practiced Method;
- Synthesis Record;
- lawful action vector;
- Expression candidate;
- Power Economy training target;
- confluence/re-authoring hypothesis;
- missing-basis list;
- warning;
- source-local boundary;
- owner-file handoff.

D05 may not directly grant:

- final Expression;
- final re-authored Expression;
- Power Economy integration;
- carrier upgrade;
- reservoir increase;
- cost reduction as permanent D03 state;
- stable confluence;
- overdraw immunity;
- corruption removal;
- final scope increase.

Those require D03 validation and, when advancement-relevant, D04 proof validation.

### D03/D05-L2 — Research Track to Expression Candidate pipeline

A D05 Research Track may produce an Expression candidate only after it identifies:

- desired outcome;
- current basis;
- missing basis;
- research mode;
- inputs;
- risk posture;
- relevant competencies and methods;
- owner handoffs.

D03 then validates:

- Expression Grade;
- Scope Profile;
- Effect Flags;
- Power Economy requirements;
- carrier/burden fit;
- cost profile;
- partition interaction;
- confluence safety;
- instability/backlash risk;
- allowed tier band;
- source-local status.

Research output is therefore not a playable Expression until D03 validates it.

### D03/D05-L3 — Training Project support for Power Economy and carrier control

D05 Training Projects may support D03 growth when the target is practice-facing.

Examples:

- training safe Mistra shaping;
- practicing Zi circulation through breath discipline;
- improving Volith concentration posture;
- stabilizing Scrypta inscription handwork;
- improving Pressa restraint;
- correcting harmful reservoir assumptions;
- reducing carrier strain through safe practice;
- rehearsing an Expression under controlled conditions.

Training may create progress, proof, reduced risk, or future Development Pick eligibility. It does not itself raise D03 capacity, partition, or carrier state unless D03 accepts the output.

### D03/D05-L4 — Synthesis is not confluence authorization

A D05 Synthesis Record may show that multiple competencies, methods, tools, Power Economy theories, or research outputs interact. This may support D03 confluence or re-authoring review.

It does not automatically make multi-economy confluence stable.

D03 must still validate:

- compatibility;
- Dominant/Secondary/Tertiary roles;
- fractional reservoir impact;
- cost split;
- stability state;
- carrier burden;
- overdraw/backlash risk;
- expression scope;
- proof or tier requirement.

### D03/D05-L5 — Methods can become Expression candidates

A D05 Method may become a D03 Expression candidate when it gains D03-facing properties:

- Power Economy cost;
- carrier burden;
- retained/inscribed state;
- ascendant/supernatural/energy effect;
- scope beyond ordinary competency;
- formal activation conditions;
- overdraw/backlash risk;
- confluence/re-authoring behavior.

Until then, it remains a D05 Method.

### D03/D05-L6 — Competency relevance can affect validation posture

D05 relevance may affect D03 validation, but it should usually affect posture, not skip validation.

Possible D03-facing effects:

- lower risk posture for a controlled attempt;
- clearer missing-basis list;
- better cost estimate;
- reduced material waste;
- safer prototype;
- improved information yield;
- reduced misread risk;
- proof generation;
- training progress;
- research progress;
- access relevance.

Relevance should not automatically grant final advantage, cost reduction, or scope increase unless D03 permits it.

### D03/D05-L7 — Advancement Track routing for D03 tracks

D03 owns advancement tracks where the primary growth object is:

- Power Economy capacity;
- reservoir depth;
- reservoir purity/refinement;
- carrier tolerance;
- partition stability;
- Expression burden;
- Expression inscription capacity;
- cost efficiency;
- recharge/recovery behavior;
- confluence stability;
- internal channel/node equivalent;
- overdraw tolerance;
- resource-facing corruption/instability.

D05 may record training/research/proof inputs and route the track to D03, but D03 validates the track.

## 3. Core D04/D05 interface laws

### D04/D05-L1 — D05 records can generate proof, but D04 weighs proof

D05 records may become proof inputs when they involve meaningful action, training, research, mastery, method formation, synthesis, teaching, route pressure, or profession-shaping events.

D04 decides:

- whether the record is proof-bearing;
- proof family;
- proof weight;
- contextual proof weight;
- whether it binds, transforms, references, or merely records proof;
- whether it satisfies a gate.

### D04/D05-L2 — Competency proof is not automatically advancement proof

A competency rank increase, training project, or research track is not automatically proof for level, tier, breakthrough, Awakening, or capstone.

It becomes D04-relevant when it changes capability, identity, route pressure, cost, consequence, world-state, risk, mastery, or meaningful direction.

### D04/D05-L3 — Professions generate Awakening pressure, not automatic routes

A Profession Record may feed Awakening Proof, Dynamic Triadic Choice, route pressure, title seeds, Development Pick targets, and capstone proof.

It does not automatically become Path/Class/Dao. D04 evaluates proof and D06 formalizes route identity.

### D04/D05-L4 — D05 Advancement Tracks route into D04 but do not replace D04 axes

D05-08 defines Advancement Tracks as general growth lanes. D04 should use this routing grammar to refine D04-04 Advancement Axis Registry.

D04 owns proof-facing axis handling, layer grants, tier authorization, and advancement gates. D05 owns competency-based tracks and routes other tracks to their owners.

### D04/D05-L5 — Development Picks require owner validation

A Development Pick may target a D05 track only if the target has a lawful D05 form and D04 validates the spend.

Examples:

- improve a Training Project;
- unlock a specialization;
- formalize a method;
- bind proof to a profession track;
- preserve a research-derived action vector;
- stabilize route pressure for later D06 review.

A visible track is not automatically a legal Development Pick target.

### D04/D05-L6 — Breakthroughs may use D05 basis but remain D04-owned

D05 can supply:

- lawful attempt vector;
- training support;
- research basis;
- method seed;
- synthesis record;
- profession proof;
- mastery proof;
- harmful synthesis or dangerous research risk.

D04 still decides breakthrough eligibility, risk posture, response menu, resolution mode, payload, failure consequences, and state deltas.

### D04/D05-L7 — Capstone must admit D05 proof diversity

Capstone proof must explicitly support:

- profession mastery;
- teaching lineage;
- research culmination;
- synthesis formalization;
- knowledge completion;
- method embodiment;
- noncombat mastery;
- impossible craft/service/labor routes;
- D05-owned Advancement Tracks.

This preserves noncombatant and mundane-origin capstones.

# Astra Ascension — D02/D05 Relevance Cross-Check Amendment

Version: v0.1  
Status: optional but recommended cross-check / pre-D06  
Applies to: D02 Resolution and Consequence Model, D02-A Dice Interface Amendment  
Source pressure: D05 Relevance Handling for Checks, Competency Records, Research/Training, and Lawful Action Vectors

---

## 1. Purpose

D02 already defines the d20 resolution structure, outcome lanes, criticals, cost commitment posture, and the principle that relevant skill, profession, special knowledge, technology, training, tools, and context may affect advantage, disadvantage, modifiers, cost, and consequence.

D05 now defines what relevance means.

This cross-check clarifies the D02/D05 boundary:

D05 classifies relevance. D02 applies resolution.

---

## 2. No full D02 rewrite required

D05 does not require a D02 redesign.

D02 remains the owner of:

- d20 roll procedure;
- natural 1 and natural 20 handling;
- temporary technical outcome lanes;
- margin bands;
- declared cost and risk;
- advantage/disadvantage as roll posture;
- modifiers as resolution inputs;
- consequence lane interpretation;
- assessment action structure.

D05 now supplies:

- relevance categories;
- relevance strength bands;
- one-primary-roll-posture rule;
- secondary effects;
- access relevance;
- harmful/invalid relevance;
- meaningful relevance records;
- lawful action-vector support.

---

## 3. Core D02/D05 interface laws

### D02/D05-L1 — D05 classifies, D02 resolves

For a check, D05 may classify competency relevance as:

- Direct;
- Supporting;
- Contextual;
- Interpretive;
- Preparatory;
- Recovery;
- Access;
- Harmful;
- Invalid.

D02 then applies any authorized roll effect or outcome effect.

### D02/D05-L2 — One primary roll posture benefit

A single D02 check should normally receive only one primary roll posture benefit from competencies.

Primary roll posture benefits include:

- advantage;
- disadvantage cancellation;
- major modifier;
- major difficulty adjustment.

Other relevant competencies may provide secondary effects.

This preserves D05’s anti-stacking rule.

### D02/D05-L3 — Relevance may affect non-roll outputs

D05 relevance may affect:

- information yield;
- cost;
- consequence;
- risk posture;
- access legality;
- proof generation;
- training progress;
- research progress;
- route pressure;
- hidden tag reveal;
- recovery from failure.

D02 should not treat relevance as only a bonus system.

### D02/D05-L4 — Access relevance controls whether a roll is valid

Some actions require a lawful action vector before rolling.

If the actor lacks access, D02 should route the request to one of:

- requires preparation;
- requires research;
- requires training;
- requires access;
- requires proof;
- requires owner validation;
- source-local only;
- dangerous temptation;
- blocked;
- quarantined.

D02 should not roll unsupported power declarations as if they were valid actions.

### D02/D05-L5 — Harmful relevance is possible but bounded

D05 may classify expertise as harmful when prior training creates confident error in the current context.

D02 may express this as:

- disadvantage;
- false-read risk;
- increased cost;
- increased consequence;
- misdirected research;
- harmful secondary effect.

Harmful relevance requires a real mismatch. It should not be arbitrary punishment for creativity.

### D02/D05-L6 — Relevance records are selective

D02 does not need to preserve every relevance decision.

A Relevance Record should be created when relevance affects:

- proof;
- training;
- research;
- route pressure;
- action-vector legality;
- major consequence;
- hidden-state interpretation;
- Development Pick eligibility.

Ordinary minor checks may remain ephemeral.

---

## 4. D02 outcome lane compatibility

D05 does not change the accepted D02 outcome bands.

The accepted D02 structure remains:

| D02 lane | Trigger |
|---|---:|
| Critical Success | Natural 20 |
| Exceptional Success | Margin +5 or higher |
| Clean Success | Margin 0 to +4 |
| Partial Result | Margin -1 |
| Mixed Result | Margin -2 to -3 |
| Failure with Consequence | Margin -4 to -7 |
| Severe Failure | Margin -8 or worse |
| Critical Failure | Natural 1 |

D05 relevance can modify posture, cost, consequence, access, and information yield, but it does not change these lane definitions.

## 5. D02 check procedure amendment

Recommended D02 check procedure after D05:

1. Player declares action and desired outcome.
2. System/GM identifies whether the action is currently lawful.
3. If access is uncertain, D05 Access Relevance or Research/Training classification is applied.
4. Player declares cost/risk if required.
5. Relevant competencies are claimed or inferred.
6. D05 classifies relevance type and strength.
7. One primary roll posture benefit is selected.
8. Secondary relevance effects are assigned if appropriate.
9. D02 performs d20 resolution.
10. D02 outcome lane is interpreted.
11. D05/D03/D04/D06/etc. owner handoffs are recorded if the action created proof, research, training, method, Expression candidate, route pressure, or other durable state.

## 7. Examples

D02 application:

### 7.2 Unsupported spoken instant-kill desire

D05 classification:

- No current lawful action vector unless the actor has sound, compulsion, relic, route, entity contact, technique, Expression, or source-local basis.

D02 application:

- no standard power roll;
- route to requires research, requires access, dangerous temptation, blocked, or quarantined;

### 7.3 Wrong expertise

A mundane doctor examines a spirit wound and applies ordinary trauma assumptions.

D05 classification:

- Medicine may be Supporting or Harmfully Relevant depending context.
- Spirit taxonomy or Esoteric/Occult knowledge may be Access or Interpretive relevance.

D02 application:

- harmful relevance may increase misread risk or consequence if the actor confidently proceeds with false assumptions.
- It should not be punitive if the actor probes cautiously.
