# Astra Ascension — D03/D05 Reconciliation Amendment

Version: v0.1  
Status: proposed amendment / required before D06  
Applies to: D03 Resource, Power Economy, Expression, Carrier, Overdraw, and Runtime State Doctrine  
Source pressure: D05 Competency, Profession, Knowledge, Training, Research, Synthesis, and Advancement Track Doctrine

---

## 1. Purpose

D05 introduced competency, profession, training, research, mastery, method, synthesis, relevance, records, and advancement-track routing doctrine. D03 must now acknowledge how those structures feed Power Economy and Expression systems without allowing D05 to authorize final D03 mechanics.

This amendment does not redesign D03. It adds controlled interfaces.

The core reconciliation question is:

How do D05 competencies, training projects, research tracks, methods, synthesis records, and advancement tracks support D03 Power Economy and Expression development without bypassing D03 validation?

---

## 2. Amendment status

D03 remains the owner of:

- Power Economy access, integration, and compatibility;
- reservoir partitioning;
- carrier tolerance;
- Expression authoring and re-authoring;
- Expression validation;
- Expression inscription and burden;
- overdraw, backlash, instability, and resource-facing corruption;
- D03 runtime state requirements.

D05 now becomes an input layer for:

- lawful action-vector creation;
- current basis and missing-basis identification;
- training toward Power Economy control;
- research toward Expression candidates;
- method seeds that may become Expressions or Techniques;
- synthesis records that may feed re-authoring or confluence;
- competency relevance affecting cost, stability, risk, information yield, and validation posture.

D05 does not directly change Power Economy, Expression, carrier, reservoir, cost, burden, or overdraw state. D03 validates those changes.

---

## 3. Core D03/D05 interface laws

### D03/D05-L1 — D05 creates basis, not final authorization

A D05 record may establish why an actor has a lawful basis to attempt, research, train toward, or prototype a D03-facing outcome.

D05 may produce:

- Research Track;
- Training Project;
- Method Seed;
- Practiced Method;
- Synthesis Record;
- lawful action vector;
- Expression candidate;
- Power Economy training target;
- confluence/re-authoring hypothesis;
- missing-basis list;
- warning;
- source-local boundary;
- owner-file handoff.

D05 may not directly grant:

- final Expression;
- final re-authored Expression;
- Power Economy integration;
- carrier upgrade;
- reservoir increase;
- cost reduction as permanent D03 state;
- stable confluence;
- overdraw immunity;
- corruption removal;
- final scope increase.

Those require D03 validation and, when advancement-relevant, D04 proof validation.

### D03/D05-L2 — Research Track to Expression Candidate pipeline

A D05 Research Track may produce an Expression candidate only after it identifies:

- desired outcome;
- current basis;
- missing basis;
- research mode;
- inputs;
- risk posture;
- relevant competencies and methods;
- owner handoffs.

D03 then validates:

- Expression Grade;
- Scope Profile;
- Effect Flags;
- Power Economy requirements;
- carrier/burden fit;
- cost profile;
- partition interaction;
- confluence safety;
- instability/backlash risk;
- allowed tier band;
- source-local status.

Research output is therefore not a playable Expression until D03 validates it.

### D03/D05-L3 — Training Project support for Power Economy and carrier control

D05 Training Projects may support D03 growth when the target is practice-facing.

Examples:

- training safe Mistra shaping;
- practicing Zi circulation through breath discipline;
- improving Volith concentration posture;
- stabilizing Scrypta inscription handwork;
- improving Pressa restraint;
- correcting harmful reservoir assumptions;
- reducing carrier strain through safe practice;
- rehearsing an Expression under controlled conditions.

Training may create progress, proof, reduced risk, or future Development Pick eligibility. It does not itself raise D03 capacity, partition, or carrier state unless D03 accepts the output.

### D03/D05-L4 — Synthesis is not confluence authorization

A D05 Synthesis Record may show that multiple competencies, methods, tools, Power Economy theories, or research outputs interact. This may support D03 confluence or re-authoring review.

It does not automatically make multi-economy confluence stable.

D03 must still validate:

- compatibility;
- Dominant/Secondary/Tertiary roles;
- fractional reservoir impact;
- cost split;
- stability state;
- carrier burden;
- overdraw/backlash risk;
- expression scope;
- proof or tier requirement.

### D03/D05-L5 — Methods can become Expression candidates

A D05 Method may become a D03 Expression candidate when it gains D03-facing properties:

- Power Economy cost;
- carrier burden;
- retained/inscribed state;
- ascendant/supernatural/energy effect;
- scope beyond ordinary competency;
- formal activation conditions;
- overdraw/backlash risk;
- confluence/re-authoring behavior.

Until then, it remains a D05 Method.

### D03/D05-L6 — Competency relevance can affect validation posture

D05 relevance may affect D03 validation, but it should usually affect posture, not skip validation.

Possible D03-facing effects:

- lower risk posture for a controlled attempt;
- clearer missing-basis list;
- better cost estimate;
- reduced material waste;
- safer prototype;
- improved information yield;
- reduced misread risk;
- proof generation;
- training progress;
- research progress;
- access relevance.

Relevance should not automatically grant final advantage, cost reduction, or scope increase unless D03 permits it.

### D03/D05-L7 — Advancement Track routing for D03 tracks

D03 owns advancement tracks where the primary growth object is:

- Power Economy capacity;
- reservoir depth;
- reservoir purity/refinement;
- carrier tolerance;
- partition stability;
- Expression burden;
- Expression inscription capacity;
- cost efficiency;
- recharge/recovery behavior;
- confluence stability;
- internal channel/node equivalent;
- overdraw tolerance;
- resource-facing corruption/instability.

D05 may record training/research/proof inputs and route the track to D03, but D03 validates the track.

### 4.5 Amend `D03_04_expression_authoring_and_reauthoring.md`

Required pipeline:

2. D05 classifies current basis and missing basis.
3. D05 opens Research Track, Training Project, Method Seed, or Synthesis Record.
4. D05 may output Expression candidate.
5. D03 validates grade, scope, flags, economy, cost, carrier, burden, risk, tier, and source-local status.

7. D06 validates route/Technique implications if present.

Recommended insertion:

```markdown
## D05-to-D03 Expression Candidate Pipeline

A desired outcome does not become an attempted Expression merely because the player describes it. If the outcome is not currently lawful, D05 may classify it as requiring research, training, access, proof, owner validation, source-local handling, or quarantine. D05 may then produce an Expression candidate. D03 validates whether the candidate can become a known, prototype, inscribed, practiced, or active Expression.
```

## DDR Fragment — D03/D05 Reconciliation

Status: proposed amendment

Design question:
- How should D03 integrate D05 competency, training, research, method, synthesis, and advancement-track doctrine without surrendering D03 ownership over Power Economies and Expressions?

Current working answer:
- D05 becomes the basis/candidate/support layer for D03. D05 may create Research Tracks, Training Projects, Method Seeds, Synthesis Records, lawful action vectors, and Expression candidates. D03 validates all final Power Economy, Expression, carrier, cost, burden, confluence, inscription, overdraw, backlash, and resource-facing recovery mechanics.

Astra-native principle:
- Experimentation and practiced competence can open lawful power-development paths, but power must still pass through cost, carrier, economy, and validation gates.

Mechanical implication:
- Add D05-to-D03 Expression Candidate Pipeline.
- Add D05 basis checks to Expression validation.
- Add D05 support to training, partition, carrier, and confluence preparation.
- Add D05 refs to D03 runtime state.

Player-facing implication:
- Players can research, train, and synthesize toward new Expressions without receiving unsupported powers.
- Mundane and professional characters can build lawful routes toward D03-facing mechanics.

Conversion implication:
- Donor spell research, technique training, energy channel development, crafting-powered abilities, and hybrid power systems can map through D05 support and D03 validation.

Runtime implication:
- Backend must distinguish D05 candidates/support records from D03 validated Expression and Power Economy state.

Affected files:
- D03_00 through D03_08.

Decision needed:
- Accept and apply this D03 amendment before D06 begins.
