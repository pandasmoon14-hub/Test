# A02 Source Fields and Magic-Technology Relation

## 1. Purpose and status
- File ID: A02
- Layer: 1_setting_spine
- Phase: 1A
- Status: draft
- Purpose: Define Source Fields and the structural relation between high magic, high technology, psionics, divine power, cultivation force, computation, biotech, and dimensional energy without importing donor-law mechanics as Astra defaults.

## 2. What this file owns
- Source Field taxonomy boundaries for doctrine-level setting interpretation.
- Relation matrix posture for magic, technology, psionics, divine, cultivation, computation, biotech, and dimensional-energy categories.
- Integration constraints for high-tech/high-magic coexistence across donor conversion pressure.
- Hybridization guardrails that preserve doctrine boundaries without defining runtime systems.

## 3. What this file must not own
- Specific spells.
- Specific techniques.
- Specific powers.
- Specific devices.
- Specific cyberware or implants.
- Specific relics or named assets.
- Gods or factions as canon.
- Specific planets or planes as canon.
- Resource pools, costs, recharge, or backlash mechanics.
- Action economy, damage rules, or progression systems.
- Cultivation stage ladders.
- Runtime state.
- K01 lexicon governance.
- C02/C03/C04 schema drafting.
- A03/A04/A05/A07b/A10/A12 doctrine drafting.

## 4. Required definitions
- **Source Field**: a doctrine-level origin-domain category for extraordinary and advanced phenomena, independent from donor naming.
- **Magic domain posture**: Source Field relation category describing arcane/sorcerous-style manifestations without importing donor spell systems.
- **Technology domain posture**: Source Field relation category describing engineered/systemic manifestations without forcing mundanity-only assumptions.
- **Psionic domain posture**: Source Field relation category for cognition/intent-mediated manifestations.
- **Divine domain posture**: Source Field relation category for transcendent/faith-authorized manifestations without declaring deity canon.
- **Cultivation-force domain posture**: Source Field relation category for refinement/attunement-style manifestation families without stage mechanics.
- **Computational domain posture**: Source Field relation category for algorithmic/symbolic inference or control manifestations.
- **Biotech domain posture**: Source Field relation category for bioengineered/living-system mediation.
- **Dimensional-energy posture**: Source Field relation category for cross-dimensional energetic mediation.
- **Hybrid manifestation**: a cross-field expression requiring explicit boundary review and non-free normalization.
- **Source-local manifestation record**: donor-anchored manifestation retained with provenance and bounded authority.
- **Escalated source-field contradiction**: unresolved relation conflict requiring formal doctrine review.

## 5. Core doctrine rules
- A02 defines Source Field taxonomy and relation posture only.
- A02 remains `A02_source_fields_magic_technology_relation.md`; do not rename A02.
- K01 remains separate; lexicon governance is not moved into A02.
- A02 is draft doctrine and is not current.
- A02 does not define runtime state, combat mechanics, economy loops, or advancement progression.

## 6. Conversion mapping rules
- **Direct field mapping**: donor manifestation maps to one Source Field posture with no contradiction.
- **Normalized field mapping**: donor manifestation maps after transparent normalization and provenance notes.
- **Multi-field tagged mapping**: manifestation carries multiple Source Field tags when relation pressure demands layered interpretation.
- **Source-local retention**: donor-specific manifestation remains source-local when doctrine-safe normalization is not available.
- **Quarantine and escalation**: contradictory or ambiguous manifestation is quarantined and escalated rather than invented into canon.

## 7. Source-local handling
- Donor manifestations may remain source-local with source anchors and conversion provenance.
- Source-local retention does not auto-promote donor terms into Astra canon.
- Named donor powers, devices, and metaphysical claims remain local unless later canon review accepts them.
- Recurrent donor patterns can inform later doctrine review without bypassing A02 boundaries.
- Conflicting manifestation claims are escalated instead of flattened into single-model assumptions.

## 8. Donor pressure absorbed
- Pressure to force magic and technology into permanently isolated silos.
- Pressure to collapse magic into pure technology equivalence.
- Pressure to force technology as strictly mundane and non-Source-mediated.
- Pressure to import donor cultivation-force ladders as default progression law.
- Pressure to hard-separate psionic/divine/computational/biotech expressions into mutually exclusive metaphysics.

## 9. Hard refusals / rejected imports
- Magic and technology are not treated as permanently unrelated silos.
- Magic is not globally reduced to technology identity.
- Technology is not globally forced to mundane-only framing.
- Donor spell-slot, essence, or equivalent resource economies are not Astra defaults.
- Hybridization is not declared unconstrained or free of review boundaries.

## 10. Escalation triggers
- New Source Field interaction type is required beyond current taxonomy.
- Relation mapping creates direct contradiction with A01 cosmology boundaries.
- Conversion pressure requests resource costs, recharge, backlash, damage, or action economy ownership.
- Proposals attempt to move K01 governance or downstream drafting responsibilities into A02.
