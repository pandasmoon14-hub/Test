## Context

Astra must absorb 200–400 donor sources whose sensing assumptions range from one passive score to multispectral sensor networks, magical divination, psychic contact, forensic traces, false records, stealth contests, and aggregate surveillance. Existing repository visibility structures are narrow projection and redaction fixtures, not sensing authority.

## Decision

No world fact, signal label, visibility flag, sensor reading, search declaration, contact, track, donor rule, public report, or model narration creates authoritative detection, recognition, identification, belief, knowledge, target eligibility, or disclosure by itself. Every authoritative sensing result is a purpose-, audience-, and time-scoped, version-pinned determination over frozen signal, propagation, sensor, attention, identity, epistemic, agency, behavioral, social, communication, institutional, embodiment, environmental, spatial, capability-and-action, governed-relation, quantity, scheduler, concealment, search, tracking, and fusion bases. AFQR-20 produces sensing, observation, evidence, contact, and track candidates only; AFQR-10 and AFQR-19 may accept, reject, qualify, or require revalidation, and only AFQR-01 commits accepted owner fragments. Narration follows audience-visible committed records and never expands them.

The architecture is federated across signal occurrence, propagation, sensor state, sensing tasks, acquisition/detection, recognition/identification, contact/track, search, fusion, surveillance, AFQR-10 evidence, and AFQR-19 target-contact owners. No universal perception attribute, stealth contest, visibility state, sensor equation, search action, or illusion ontology is adopted.

## Consequences

1. Detection may succeed without localization or identification.
2. False positives, false negatives, unresolved mixtures, ambiguous identities, incomplete searches, and lost tracks are lawful.
3. Concealment is modality-, observer-, stage-, purpose-, and interval-specific.
4. AFQR-20 ends in evidence/contact candidates; AFQR-10 and AFQR-19 retain downstream authority.
5. Model output requires evidence bindings and semantic validation.
6. Runtime execution remains blocked pending registries, evaluators, owner adapters, deterministic RNG, security controls, and certification fixtures.

## Rejected universal architectures

Candidates A–G are retained only as source-local or profile-level mechanisms. Candidate H is selected with the amendments recorded in the decision registry.

## Roadmap
