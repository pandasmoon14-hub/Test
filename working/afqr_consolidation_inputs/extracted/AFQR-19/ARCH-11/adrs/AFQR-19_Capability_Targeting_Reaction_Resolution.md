## Decision

No capability label, ability record, equipment grant, action declaration, target reference, dice expression, modifier, trigger, reaction claim, initiative value, combat context, donor rule, public report, or model narration possesses action-resolution or mutation authority by itself. Every authoritative action result is a purpose-scoped, version-pinned, bitemporal determination over frozen capability, access, readiness, legality, identity, epistemic, agency, behavioral, social, communication, institutional, embodiment, environmental, spatial, governed-relation, quantity, scheduler, target, contest, reaction, and resolution bases. AFQR-19 may produce resolution receipts and owner-specific effect candidates only; downstream owners may accept, reject, attenuate, transform, or quarantine those candidates, and only AFQR-01 commits accepted owner fragments. Narration follows audience-visible committed results and never expands them.

AFQR-19 owns capability readiness determinations, relational action opportunities, target binding and layered eligibility, resolver selection and execution receipts, contest state, trigger/reaction windows, bounded reaction ordering, simultaneous-action conflict graphs, conflict-context participation, and complete non-mutating effect candidates. It does not own behavioral action selection, spatial movement, environmental state, bodily harm, resource settlement, social or institutional consequences, identity continuity, or narration authority.

## Consequences

- No universal dice, initiative, action economy, target grammar, combat round, attack formula or critical rule is adopted.
- Backend RNG uses reserved streams and immutable draw receipts.
- Reactions are bounded protocols rather than recursive free actions.
- Resolution outcomes are not committed effects; downstream owners may reject or transform candidates.
- Combat is a specialized conflict context using ordinary action authority.
- All generalized execution remains blocked pending registries, validators and certification fixtures.
- AFQR-20 should resolve sensing, attention, concealment, detection and information acquisition before generalized targeting.
