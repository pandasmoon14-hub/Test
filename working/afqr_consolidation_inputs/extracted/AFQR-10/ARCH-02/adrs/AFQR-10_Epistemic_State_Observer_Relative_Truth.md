## Central law

> Authoritative world truth and every observer-relative epistemic state are separately owned, versioned, and append-only. They may be connected only by registered observation, evidence, testimony, communication, inference, memory, and disclosure receipts. No observation, belief, memory, rumor, map, official record, or model output changes truth; no truth enters an observer or model projection without a registered visibility-safe path.

## Decision

Astra adopts a federated proposition registry, bitemporal authoritative fact layer, typed epistemic subjects, immutable observation/evidence/testimony records, observer-owned belief and memory state, profile-qualified knowledge, plural uncertainty, pure registered inference and revision, explicit secrecy/disclosure/deception/rumor transitions, finite group/common-knowledge certificates, and audience/purpose-scoped projections.

- Source-local divination, omniscience, telepathy, hive minds, and memory alteration require registered profiles.
